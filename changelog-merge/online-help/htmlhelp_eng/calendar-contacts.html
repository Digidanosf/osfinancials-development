  1. Calendar / Planner

    Contacts - Calendar

    In osFinancials, the Calendar feature provides the ability to manage contacts, ensuring that any changes made to contact information are reflected across the system. The following options on the Default ribbon allow you to create, edit, or delete contacts, with all updates being synchronized with the Contacts in the Calendar:

    1. Contacts: This option allows you to access the general Contacts section, where you can manage and maintain contact information for debtors (customers/clients) and creditors (suppliers/vendors).
    2. Debtors - Contacts tab: This tab specifically focuses on managing contacts associated with debtor accounts. You can add, edit, or delete contacts linked to debtor accounts, ensuring that the contact details are up to date.
    3. Creditors - Contacts tab: Similar to the Debtors - Contacts tab, this tab is dedicated to managing contacts associated with creditor accounts. You can modify the contact information for your suppliers/vendors to maintain accurate records.

    By utilizing these options, you can conveniently manage contacts within osFinancials, ensuring that all contact information remains consistent and accessible across different sections in a Set of Books.

    You may print a "Contact listing" report for all debtors (customers/clients) as well as contacts for creditors (suppliers / vendors) from Contacts option on the Default ribbon. Additionally "Contact listing" reports may be printed in the following options on the Reports ribbon: 

    • Contact listing - This report will list all contacts for debtors (customers/clients) as well as contacts for creditors (suppliers/vendors). It offers a consolidated view of all contacts in your Set of Books.
    • Reports → Debtors → Listing - Contacts - This report specifically focuses on contacts related to debtors (customers/clients). It provides a listing of contacts associated with your debtor accounts.
    • Reports → Creditors → Listing - Contacts - This report specifically lists contacts related to creditors (suppliers/vendors). It presents a comprehensive view of contacts associated with your creditor accounts.
