    Print single item - Transaction details

    Transaction details - Stock grid reports 

    The "Transaction details" report list the document date (and time, if available) document number, document type, quantities, salesperson and document reference for each stock item.

    You may select the "Documents" and the "Stock In / Out" reports on the Reports → Stock → Stock In / Out menu (Reports ribbon) to print stock transactions.  You may select a specific stock item or a range of stock items; or all stock items linked to a specific Stock reporting group 1.
