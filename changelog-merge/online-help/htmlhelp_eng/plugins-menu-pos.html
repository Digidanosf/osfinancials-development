    Customer on hold

    Customer on hold - Manual - Shop - Licence : Once-off - 

    Enhance your Point-of-Sale (POS) experience with our Customer on Hold Plugin. Designed for the Standard free POS module, this plugin allows you to put POS invoices on hold, enabling you to add products to the invoice later, making it ideal for businesses like bars or restaurants.

    Key Features:

      • Put POS invoices on hold for later modification.
      • Ideal for bars, restaurants, and similar businesses.
      • Specifically designed for the Standard free POS module.

    Streamline your POS operations and improve customer service with the Customer on Hold Plugin, exclusively available for the Standard free POS module.

    The HorecaPOS and RetailPOS system, do not support this plugin.

    HorecaPOS addition quantity on hand adjustment

    HorecaPOS addition quantity on hand adjustment - Manual - Shop - Licence : Annual subscription -

    The HorecaPOS Addition Quantity on Hand Adjustment Plugin, available through an annual subscription, empowers employees to conveniently update the quantity on hand for specific items within the HorecaPOS system. This functionality enables seamless management of inventory levels, allowing employees to accurately track stock movements in real-time. For instance, employees can input the quantity of pies retrieved from the freezer or steaks prepared for baking, ensuring that inventory records remain up-to-date and reflective of actual stock levels.

    POSDisplay Plugin

    POSDisplay Plugin - Manual - Shop - Licence : Once-off - 

    Documentation : Help topic 

    The POS Display plugin enhances the customer experience during checkout by utilizing a second monitor (minimum 600x800 resolution) to display detailed invoices. Customers can easily view their purchases and the corresponding prices per item, as well as the total amount due.

    Additionally, the plugin enables you to showcase product images on the second monitor while customers wait to check out. These images can be set to rotate at specified intervals, attracting attention to special offers or promotions. Rotation options include displaying images at the top, left, or right side of the invoices.

    POS Points

    POS Points - Customer loyalty points - Manual - Shop - Licence: Once-off 

    Boost customer loyalty with our POS Points Plugin. With a one-time licence, easily set up a customer loyalty program where a percentage of each sale is converted into points. Customers accumulate points with each purchase, which can then be redeemed for discounts on future purchases.

    Key Features:

      • Set percentage of sales to be converted into points
      • Accumulate points with each purchase
      • Redeem points for discounts on future purchases
      • Enhance customer loyalty and satisfaction

    With the POS Points Plugin, you can incentivize repeat purchases and foster long-term relationships with your customers, ultimately driving business growth and success.
