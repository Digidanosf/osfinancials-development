    Currencies (Reports menu)

    The "Currencies - Listing" report will list the currencies (added / edited in the Setup → Currencies menu on the Setup ribbon). 

    You may process Multi-currency documents (i.e. invoices, credit notes and quotes for debtors (customers / clients) or purchases, supplier returns and orders for creditors (suppliers). The selected currency will be printed in the document layout files.

    osFinancials supports a wide range of currencies, with a total of 180 available currency rates. As these currency rates can fluctuate daily, and sometimes even hourly, users must ensure they regularly update and maintain their currency list before processing any documents. This ensures that accurate exchange rates are used when dealing with foreign currencies on sales documents (invoices, credit notes, and quotes) or purchase documents (purchases, supplier returns, and orders).

    Currencies - Listing report options

    To print Currencies listing reports: 

    1. On the Reports ribbon, select Reports → Currencies. 

           

    1. Select the Sequence (i.e. ID (record number), Description or Rate).
    2. Click on the OK button.
