  1. Stock (Inventory / Products)

    Delete stock items

    You may delete a stock item, if it has no transactions and/or balances. 

    You may disable a stock item to hide the stock item from the stock lookup when processing documents. Disabled stock items will still be included on the stock lookup when printing reports. 

    You may need to do this, if you are no longer use the stock item, the stock item is discontinued, etc.

    Delete items

    To delete a stock item:

    1. On the Stock items, select the stock item, and click on the Delete button. A confirmation message is displayed: 

    "Are you sure you want to delete this stock item?" 

    Make sure absolutely sure that the correct stock item is selected.
    1. Click on the Yes button. The stock item will be permanently removed (deleted).

    Disable items

    If you cannot delete a stock item, if it has no transactions and/or balances you may disable or set the stock item as inactive.

    Advantages of Disabling or Setting a Stock Item to Inactive:

    1. Organization: Disabling stock items that are no longer in use such as discontinued items, helps keep your inventory database organized and decluttered.
    2. Accuracy: By removing inactive items from the selection options, you reduce the chances of users mistakenly selecting them for new transactions.
    3. Focus: Keeping only active items visible can streamline the selection process for users, allowing them to focus on items that are relevant to current operations.
    4. Compliance: In some cases, regulatory or internal compliance requirements may necessitate the removal of certain items from active circulation. Disabling them ensures adherence to these requirements. 

    Ultimately, the decision to disable or set a stock item to inactive should be made based on factors such as current usage, future plans, and organizational policies, while considering the balance between organization and operational efficiency.

    When processing sales and purchase documents, 

    To disable a stock item:

    1. If the stock item has transactions and/or balances, you cannot delete it. An information message is displayed: 

    "3COMP You cannot delete this stock item! It has transactions! 12 PU000004" 
    (3COMP is the stock code and PU000004 is the last processed document number as per this example).

    1. To hide the stock item from the stock item lookup when processing documents, select the stock item and click on the Edit button. 
    2. Tick the Disabled field.
    3. Click on the Save button.
