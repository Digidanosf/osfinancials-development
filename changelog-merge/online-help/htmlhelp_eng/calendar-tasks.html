  1. Calendar / Planner

    Tasks - Calendar

    In osFinancials, the Calendar feature includes the ability to manage tasks or a to-do list. You can add, edit, or delete tasks according to your requirements. When creating a task, you can specify a completion date to indicate when the task should be completed.

    Additionally, you have the option to include notes or additional details for each task. These notes can serve as reminders for additional steps, procedures, contact information such as phone numbers, or any other relevant information related to the task.

    By utilising the task management functionality in the osFinancials Calendar, you can stay organized and keep track of your to-do list efficiently. This feature helps you manage and prioritize your tasks effectively, ensuring that important activities are completed within the desired timeframe.

    You may add, edit, or delete tasks (to do list), and set a completion date when the tasks should be completed. You may also add some notes to each task to remind you of additional steps, procedures, phone numbers, etc. 

    All tasks (excluding the deleted tasks, will display as follows:

    • Expired or overdue tasks - Will display in a red font colour.
    • Tasks marked as completed - The field is ticked and the task displays a stripe through it. The task will also be displayed in a grey font.
