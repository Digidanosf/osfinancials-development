    Price Agreement Plugin

    Price Agreement Plugin - Manual - Shop - Licence : Once-off 

    Documentation : Help documentation 

    Translations : Outstanding    

    Maximize your pricing flexibility and competitiveness with our Price Agreement Plugin. With a convenient one-time licence, unlock the ability to offer quantity discounts on four levels, tailored to individual debtors or creditors.

    Define your price agreements effortlessly, whether it's discounts on default sales prices, percentages based on cost prices, or specific amounts per item. Set special prices for select customers or suppliers, overriding default product prices, and include additional details like supplier product numbers and informational text.

    Enjoy the convenience of setting start and end dates for agreements, and link prices from stock groups to debtor groups for streamlined management of larger groups. Elevate your pricing strategy and enhance customer relationships with the Price Agreement Plugin.

    Subscriptions

    Subscriptions - Manual - Shop - Licence : Annual licence - 

    Documentation : Help documentation 

    Translations : Outstanding    

    Elevate your subscription-based business with our Subscriptions Plugin. Perfect for magazine publishers, gymnasiums, rental services, and more, this plugin ensures you never miss charging your members for their subscriptions again.

    With the Subscriptions Plugin, effortlessly manage member details such as purchased products/services, repeating frequencies, and warning periods for renewal notices. Stay on top of renewals with automatic reminders and easily generate invoices with just a click.

    Track renewal dates, invoicing history, and subscription details at a glance, and streamline communication with automated invoice emails. Plus, efficiently search for specific products/services and view subscription details for each member.

    Optimize your subscription management process and enhance customer satisfaction with the Subscriptions Plugin.
