    Specialised journals

    Payroll journal: This journal type can be used specifically for recording payroll entries, including salary expenses, tax withholdings, benefits, and other related transactions. It allows for accurate tracking and reporting of payroll-related costs.

    Fixed assets journal: By creating a custom journal type for fixed assets, you can record transactions related to asset acquisition, depreciation, disposals, and any other changes in the fixed asset register. This ensures proper tracking of asset values and facilitates compliance with accounting standards and regulatory requirements.

    Accruals journal: An accruals journal can be used to record accrual-based adjustments, such as recognizing revenue or expenses that have been incurred but not yet recorded in the general ledger. This journal type helps ensure accurate matching of revenues and expenses in the appropriate accounting periods.

    Inventory adjustment journal: This journal type allows for the recording of inventory adjustments, such as write-offs, write-downs, and any changes in the quantity or value of inventory items. It helps maintain accurate inventory records and supports proper valuation of inventory on the financial statements
