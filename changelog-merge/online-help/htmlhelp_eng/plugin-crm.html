    CRM Plugin

    Enhance your customer relationship management with our CRM plugin for osFinancials and TurboCASH. With an annual licence, gain access to powerful features designed to streamline lead management and customer interactions.

    Key Features:

    • Lead Management: Easily add and manage leads within the system for efficient tracking and follow-up.
    • Flexible Data Import: Import leads effortlessly by loading a file and defining custom data fields. Supported file formats include comma or tab-delimited files.
    • Customizable Fields: Define your own data fields based on your specific requirements. No fields are compulsory, providing flexibility in data entry.
    • Tagging System: Tag leads and existing clients for easy categorization and organization.
    • Contact Management: Record contact moments such as phone calls, emails, or meetings, enabling comprehensive communication tracking.
    • Document Integration: Attach documents to leads or client profiles using the multimedia plugin. Additionally, upload images for visual identification, such as group photos or individual portraits.
    • Visual Identification: View attached images or documents directly within the debtor profiles, facilitating quick identification and reference.

    Streamline your CRM processes, enhance lead management, and improve customer interactions with the CRM plugin for osFinancials and TurboCASH.




    CRM allows you to add leads - Easy way to (load in a file and define a definition yourself) It must be a separated file - i.e. comma or tab delimited

    non of the fields are compulsory

    you can add tags to leads and existing clients

    add contact moments - i.e. phoning him or he phones you

    add documents through multimedia plugin or a image of the person (group name photo)

    this image can then be viewed at debtors (like stock item) or in the plugin
