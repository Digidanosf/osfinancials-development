    Debtor accounts - Appointments tab

    In osFinancials5, the "Debtor accounts - Appointments" tab allows you to view and manage appointments specifically related to a selected debtor (customer/client) account. This tab provides a centralised view of all scheduled debtor appointments that have been added in the Calendar (Default ribbon).

    By accessing the "Debtor accounts - Appointments" tab, you will be able to see a comprehensive list of all debtor appointments/events that have been scheduled in the Calendar. This includes appointments for meetings, discussions, notes, or any other relevant activities associated with your debtor account.

    If any Bulk time entries were processed for a selected debtor (customer/client) account these entries will only be listed on the "Debtor accounts - Appointments" tab, once you generated quotes on the "Create invoices for hours" button.

    All scheduled Debtor appointments (events) added in the Calendar (Default ribbon) for the selected debtor (customer / client) account will be listed here.

    If you double click on a selected appointment, the Bulk time entry screen will be launched. You may select the dates edit reschedule, add notes, etc. and set reminders, etc.  

    When you use the Bulk time entry feature to add time entries for a selected debtor account, those entries are initially recorded but not directly listed on the "Debtor accounts - Appointments" tab. However, once you proceed to generate quotes for the hours using the "Create invoices for hours" button on the Calendar, the bulk time entries will be transformed into quotes.

    Therefore, to see the bulk time entries reflected on the "Debtor accounts - Appointments" tab, it is necessary to generate quotes for those hours using the "Create invoices for hours" button in osFinancials5.

    At this point, the quotes created from the bulk time entries will be listed on the "Debtor accounts - Appointments" tab. 

    The Repeating invoices event will not be listed in the "Debtor accounts - Appointments" tab. The Repeating invoices event will not be listed in the Reports → Calendar report. 

    You may click on the column headings (i.e. Description or Notes) to change the sort sequence from the ascending to the descending sequence.

    You may click on any of the column headings (i.e. Start time, End time, Description or Notes) and drag it to the left or right to change the sequence in which to display the columns.
