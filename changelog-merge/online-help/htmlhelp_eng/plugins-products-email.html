    Document email pro plugin Newsletter

    Document email pro plugin Newsletter - Shop - Licence : Once-off -

    The "Document email pro plugin Newsletter" plugin for osFinancials enhances your communication capabilities by allowing you to send documents via email seamlessly. This documentation specifically focuses on the newsletter functionality of the plugin, providing a detailed guide on its features and usage.

    Key Features:

    • Document Email Integration:
      • Send documents such as invoices or quotations directly via email.
      • Option to choose between HTML or plain text format.
      • Define unlimited email layouts tailored to your preferences.
      • Add custom text entries for individual customers or email recipients.
    • Email Template Management:
      • Separate email template management for easy customization.
      • Configure email data sets independently for each template.
    • SMTP Server Integration:
      • Support for SMTP server with password access for secure email delivery.
    • Recipient Management:
      • Retrieve recipients from osFinancials database for newsletter distribution.
      • Integration with E-Commerce plugin allows inclusion of users from your shop or CRM system.
    • Usage Method:
      • Document Email Creation:
        • Right-click on the document (e.g., invoice or quotation, which is sent as a pdf attachment) to initiate email creation.
        • In the second selection field of the email template that the email body text.
        • Optionally, in the shipping screen, where you also can add additional text not included in the template condition.
      • Additional Text Entry:
        • Provide extra text during email composition, not included in the template.
        • Opportunity to include personalized messages for recipients.
      • Preview and Trial:
        • Preview email in the browser before sending.
        • Send a trial version to CC/BCC email addresses for review.
      • Finalizing and Sending:
        • Review the email content before sending.
        • After sending, display confirmation message for reference.
    • Installation:
      • The plugin comes pre-installed with osFinancials and does not require separate downloads.
      • Upon payment confirmation, receive the plugin code and unlocking instructions via email.
      • Initiate plugin activation directly from osFinancials with a simple button push.
      • Activation typically occurs within one working day after payment receipt.

    The Email Pro plugin's newsletter functionality enhances your communication efficiency by integrating document emailing and newsletter distribution seamlessly within the osFinancials system. With its intuitive interface and versatile features, stay connected with your customers effortlessly.
