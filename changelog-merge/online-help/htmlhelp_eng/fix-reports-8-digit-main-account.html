    FIXED - Reports - 8x3 Digit account codes - Reports

    8x3 Digit accounts - Reports Test

    On some reports, if the Chart of Accounts consisting of 8-digit main accounts and 3-digit sub-accounts is created with the "Customise your own Set of Books (Advanced)?" option, and transactions and data is processed, the reports, seemed to have many overlapping columns and text on the printouts. In the Report designer, these reports were realigned, and properties of expressions changed, where necessary.
