    Budgets - Enter budget figures for special events

    In this example, on 1 May 2022 we need to budget 5,000.00 extra sales for a special promotion. You may enter budget figures for specific dates and/or periods to accommodate, for example, special events, fluctuations and business patterns.

    To enter budget figures for special events: 

    1. On the Reports ribbon, select Budgets.

    1. Period – Select the period “Year” (i.e. “2022/03/01” to “2023/02/28") to enter your budget figures for the selected period.
    2. Click on the Refresh button. This will list all the existing ledger accounts in your “Chart of accounts” (existing as added, edited in the Setup → Accounts (Setup ribbon)), in the Account code sequence.  
    3. Select the “Sales” account and the month “2022/05/01” - May 2022. 
    4. Reference – The default or previous reference entered, will be displayed, for example, “BUDG”. Enter “SALE“ to indicate that it is a sales event that is scheduled for May 2022; or that took place in May 2022.
    5. Description – The description as previously entered, is “Budget 2022/2023”.  Enter “Budget May 2022 – Sale”. 
    6. Only enter the additional amount with which  you need to increase the budget for May 2022. 
    7. To increase the existing amount for May 2022 from –10,000 to –15000, only enter  –5000 in the amount field (before the Apply to selection button).

    The “ - “ (minus sign), added before the amount, indicates a credit budget amount. For a debit budget amount, add the amount without the “ - “ (minus sign).

    In the case of Sales and trading stock items, is applicable, you may need to change the “Cost of of sales” account. 
    1. Click on the Apply to selection button. A confirmation message , is displayed: 

    “Add amount to selection?”

    1. Click Yes to save the increased budget figure for May 2022.
