The Retail POS plugin is designed to enhance the functionality of osFinancials specifically for retail businesses. It provides features tailored to meet the needs of the retail industry. Here are the functional requirements and features of the Retail POS plugin:

1. **Quantity Totalization**: When multiple quantities of the same item are purchased, the plugin totalizes them on a single line, simplifying the presentation of the receipt.
1. **Product Options**: The plugin supports the use of product options, allowing for customization or selection of different variations or features of a product.
1. **Additional Options**: The plugin provides the capability to handle additional options such as money handling or waste disposal stations, enabling seamless integration with specific retail requirements.
1. **Rental Functionality**: The plugin includes functionality for handling rental items, allowing businesses to manage rentals and associated transactions within the point-of-sale system.
1. **Orders on Hold**: The plugin supports placing orders on hold, allowing users to save incomplete orders and retrieve them later for further processing.
1. **Order Splitting**: Users can split orders into separate transactions, which is useful for customers making partial payments or dividing the purchase between different payment methods.
1. **Flexible Payment Methods**: The plugin enables the configuration of specific payment methods to be used at the point of sale, providing flexibility to accommodate various payment options accepted by the retail business.
1. **Price Agreements**: Retail businesses can utilize price agreements within the Retail POS system to offer special pricing or discounts to specific customers or customer groups.
1. **Real-Time Sales Reporting**: The plugin provides real-time sales results in the form of charts or graphs, allowing businesses to monitor and analyze sales performance.
1. **Touchscreen Support**: The Retail POS plugin is designed to work seamlessly with touchscreen monitors, providing an intuitive and user-friendly interface for sales transactions.
1. **Groups and Color Schemes**: Users can organize products into groups and utilize color schemes for efficient categorization and visual differentiation.
1. **Comments in Sales**: The plugin allows users to add comments or notes to sales transactions, providing additional information or instructions.
1. **Serial Numbers/Batch/Locations**: The Retail POS system supports the use of serial numbers, batch numbers, and location tracking for inventory management and tracking purposes.
1. **Real-Time Salesman** Switching: Users can switch between salesmen in real-time within the Retail POS system, facilitating seamless transition and continuity during sales transactions.
1. **RF-ID Login Control**: The plugin offers login restriction and control using RF-ID (Wireless Access) technology, enhancing security and access control.
1. **Customer Selection**: Users can choose an existing customer with each sale, allowing for personalized service and tracking of customer transactions.
1. **Prepayment Capability**: The plugin supports prepayment functionality, allowing customers to make payments in advance and providing proof of payment at the checkout.
1. **Handheld Computer (PDA) Support**: The Retail POS system is compatible with handheld computers (PDAs), enabling sales transactions directly from the shop floor, enhancing mobility and customer service.
1. **Customer Creation**: The plugin allows for customer creation directly within the POS system, facilitating efficient customer onboarding and data management.
1. **Customer Search by Postal Code**: The POS system provides a search function to find customers based on their postal code and home number, streamlining customer identification.
1. **Previous Sales/Product Search**: Users can search for previous sales or products that were already purchased within the POS system, enabling quick access to customer history and previous transactions.
1. **Barcode Selection**: When multiple barcodes match a scan, the system presents a selection screen with additional product information, assisting in accurate product identification.
1. **Change Display**: The plugin continues to display the change amount even when the cash drawer is opened, ensuring visibility and transparency during cash transactions.

Please note that the mentioned features are specific to the Retail POS plugin within osFinancials. The plugin provides a comprehensive set of functionalities to meet the specific requirements of retail businesses, streamline sales processes, and enhance customer service.
