# AI/ETL in osFinancials5.1/TurboCASH5-3 - Roadmap

The combination of **OpenAI** and **ETL (Extract, Transform, Load)** for the Imports of bank statements in the **Bank import plugin** is introduced in osFinancias5.1.0.0.233.&nbsp;

## **What is ETL?**

**ETL** stands for **Extract, Transform, Load**. It’s a process used in data integration to:

* **Extract:** Pull data from one or multiple sources (e.g., databases, files, APIs).
* **Transform:** Clean, organize, and convert the data into a consistent format.
* **Load:** Store the transformed data in a target system (e.g., data warehouse, data lake).

# Activate OpenAI in osFinancialos5.1/TurboCASH5-3&nbsp;

To activate **OpenAI** and **ETL** (**Extract, Transform, Load**) functionality in **osFinancials5.1** or **TurboCASH5-3**, follow these steps:

1. **Open the Application**:
1. Launch **osFinancials5.1** or **TurboCASH5-3** on your local machine.
1. **Navigate to Setup**:
- &nbsp;

  - **Access Company Info**: In the **Setup** menu, click on **Setup → Company info** (**Setup** ribbon). This will open a window where you can configure company-specific information and settings.
  - **Go to the Options Tab**: In the **Company Info** window, navigate to the **Options** tab. This is where you can enter your OpenAI credentials.

![Image](<lib/setup-company-info-options.png>)

* &nbsp;
  * **Enter OpenAI Credentials**:

    * **OpenAI Token**: Enter your valid OpenAI API token. This token is provided when you subscribe to OpenAI's services.
    * **OpenAI Company Key**: Enter your OpenAI company key, which is also provided as part of your OpenAI subscription.
    * **Save the Settings**:
    * Click **Save** to store your OpenAI token and OpenAI company key. Then, click **OK** to close the window.

4) **Credentials Storage**: Your OpenAI token and company key will be securely stored in the **Registry** of your local machine under the **osFinancials5** folder. This allows the credentials to be used across any **Set of Books** in both **osFinancials5.1** and **TurboCASH5-3**.
4) **Verification**: Once the credentials are saved, the OpenAI and ETL functionalities should be activated and ready for use within the application.

**Important Notes**:

* Ensure that your OpenAI token and company key are valid and active. If you encounter any issues, double-check the credentials and your OpenAI subscription status.
* The credentials are stored locally on your machine, so if you move to a different computer, you will need to re-enter the credentials on that machine.

By following these steps, you should be able to successfully activate OpenAI and ETL functionalities in **osFinancials5.1** or **TurboCASH5-3**.
