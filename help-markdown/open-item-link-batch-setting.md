# Open item link - Disable / Enable in batches

***

By default, the "*Open item selection*" feature is enabled in batches. The last column, i.e. "*Open item*", will be available in "*Batch entry*" screens. 

If the "*Open item*" column is displayed, and you wish to disable open item processing, you may disable this feature. 

**To disable open item in batches:**

1. On the "*Batch entry*" screen, click **F10: Setup**. 

![Image](<lib/open-item-setting-batch-active.png>)

2. On the **Advanced** tab, add a tick in the "*Not open item batch*" field.

![Image](<lib/open-item-setting-batch-deactivate.png>)

3. Click **OK**. The "*Open item*" column will be removed from the "*Batch entry*" screen.

![Image](<lib/open-item-setting-batch-deactivated.png>)
