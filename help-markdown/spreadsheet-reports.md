**Spreadsheet reports** - Reports in a Excel spreadsheet.&nbsp; These spreadsheet reports are stored in the ...\\plug\_ins\\reports\\excelreports folder.

![Image](<lib/ribbon-reports-spreadsheet-menu.png>)

The following reports will be opened in the powerful spreadsheet feature in osFinancials5/TurboCASH5:

1. [**Creditors**](<spreadsheet-reports-creditors.md>) - The **Creditors → Listing - [List postal addresses**](<creditors-list-postal-address.md>) report will be imported into the spreadsheet.&nbsp;
1. [**Debtors**](<spreadsheet-reports-debtors.md>) - The **Debtors → Listing** - [**List postal addresses**](<debtors-list-postal-address.md>) report will be imported into the spreadsheet.&nbsp;
1. [**Transactions per period**](<spreadsheet-reports-transactions.md>) - List the transactions in posted batches and documents (i.e. sales documents (Invoices and Credit notes) and Purchase documents (Purchases and Supplier returns)) for a selected period in a spreadsheet.
1. **General ledger - Budget**&nbsp;

   1. [Column balances this year - Reporting group 1](<spreadsheet-report-budget-groups.md>)&nbsp;
   1. [Column balances this year - This year](<spreadsheet-report-budget-this-y.md>)&nbsp;

1. **General ledger (Transaction totals)**

   1. [Column balances this year - Reporting group 1](<spreadsheet-report-ledger-groups.md>)&nbsp;
   1. [Column balances this year - This year Column balances](<spreadsheet-report-ledger-this-y.md>) will import the transaction totals in two (2) tabs:

      1. Column balances - The totals for each period in separate columns
      1. Settings - The totals for each period in rows per period.

1. **Sales analysis**&nbsp;

   1. [Stock movement](<spreadsheet-report-stock-movemen.md>)&nbsp; &nbsp;
   1. [Sales per period](<spreadsheet-report-sales-period.md>) &nbsp;
