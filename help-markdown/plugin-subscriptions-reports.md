# Subscription - Report

In the **Report** option, you may print four subscription reports. &nbsp;

![Image](<lib/subscriptions-reports.png>)

Select one of the following reports:&nbsp;

* **Reporting group 1** - This will print your subscriptions linked to Stock group 1.
* **Reporting group 2** - This will print your subscriptions linked to Stock group 2.
* **Subscriptions** - This will print your subscriptions.&nbsp;
* **Subscriptions list** - This will print a list of your subscriptions.&nbsp;

Click on the **Show** button.&nbsp;

The report parameter screen will be displayed for three reports (i.e. Reporting group 1, Reporting group 2, and Subscriptions list)

&nbsp;![Image](<lib/subscriptions-reports-parameters.png>)

Select the "*From date*" and "*To date*" and click **OK**.
