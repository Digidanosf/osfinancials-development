# Tax Inclusive / Exclusive - Documents

***

You may select to change the amounts to be displayed as exclusive or exclusive of Output VAT/GST/Sales Tax on all sales documents for debtors (customers / clients) (i.e. Invoices, Credit notes and Quotes) and exclusive or exclusive of Input VAT/GST/Sales Tax on all purchase documents for creditors (suppliers) (i.e. Purchase, Supplier returns and Orders).&nbsp;

The selling price amounts for debtor (customer / client) documents are automatically inserted in the documents when a stock item is selected.&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *Please take note of whether these selling prices are entered or adjusted Inclusive or Exclusive of VAT/GST/Sales Tax on **Stock items** (**Default** ribbon), or if you adjust the selling prices in the&nbsp; **Input → Adjust stock** menu&nbsp; (**Selling prices** tab) on the **Default** ribbon.*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


The purchase (cost) price amounts for creditor (supplier / vendor) documents are automatically inserted in the documents when a stock item is selected.&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *By default, the amounts are inserted or entered (if manually over-typed) Exclusive of VAT/GST/Sales Tax on the amount column of the document lines. To enter and view amounts Inclusive of VAT/GST/Sales Tax, you may select (tick) the "Default inclusive for documents" field option on **Setup → Stock information** menu (**Setup** ribbon).* ![Image](<lib/documents-tax-inclusive-setting.png>) |
| --- | --- |


# Tax Exclusive mode

**To set the Exclusive of Tax (VAT/GST/Sales tax) mode:**&nbsp;

1. Click on the **F7:Exclusive** icon. \
![Image](<lib/documents-tax-exclusive.png>)
1. The amount will change to the selected mode, and will be displayed in the transaction section, as well as at the bottom of the Document entry screen.&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | *You may press the **F7** key or click on the [**F7:Exclusive / F7:Inclusive***](<document-entry-tax-inclusive-exc.md>) *icons on the "Document entry" screen for unposted documents to toggle between the Inclusive or Exclusive modes.* |
| --- | --- |


# Tax Inclusive mode

**To set the Inclusive of Tax (VAT/GST/Sales tax) mode:**&nbsp;

1. Click on the **F7:Inclusive** icon.&nbsp;

![Image](<lib/documents-tax-inclusive.png>)

2. The amount will change to the selected mode, and will be displayed in the transaction section, as well as at the bottom of the Document entry screen.&nbsp;
