# Sales - (Reports menu)

***

Various reports are available on the **Reports → Sales** menu to analyse each of the sales documents (i.e. Quotes, Invoices and Credit notes). The reports for Invoices are as follows:![Image](<lib/reports-sales-menu.png>)

1. **Debtors** – Debtor – Invoices per period – Quantities.&nbsp;
1. **Sales** – Invoices per period – Quantities&nbsp;
1. **Items**&nbsp; - Items per period.&nbsp;
