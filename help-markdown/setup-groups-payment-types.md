# Payment types

***

Payment types - To be advised.

Some examples of reporting groups for payment types:

1. **Cash payments**: This group can include all payment types that involve cash transactions, such as cash payments made to suppliers or cash refunds issued to customers.
1. **Bank payments**: This group can include payment types that involve transactions made through bank accounts, such as electronic fund transfers, checks, or online payments.
1. **Credit card payments**: This group can include payment types that involve credit card transactions, including payments received from customers via credit card or payments made to suppliers using a company credit card.
1. **Online payment services**: This group can include payment types that involve online payment platforms or services, such as PayPal, Stripe, or other similar payment gateways.
1. **Direct debit payments**: This group can include payment types where funds are debited directly from a customer's bank account, such as recurring automatic payments or instalment plans.
1. **Wire transfers**: This group can include payment types that involve wire transfers, typically used for international transactions or large-value payments.
1. **Voucher payments**: This group can include payment types that involve the use of vouchers or gift cards, such as redeeming customer vouchers or making supplier payments using prepaid vouchers.
1. **Payment plan instalments**: This group can include payment types that involve instalment payment plans, allowing customers to pay for products or services in multiple instalments over a specified period.

These are just a few examples, and the actual reporting groups for payment types can be customized and tailored to fit the specific needs and requirements of a business.
