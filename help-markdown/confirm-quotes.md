# Confirm Quotes

***

Once the debtor (customer / client) accepts the Quote, it can then be confirmed and converted to an Invoice. Once a Quote is confirmed and converted to an Invoice, you may need to edit the Invoice, before you update (post) the Invoice to the ledger.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *The debtor (customer / client) may, when accepting the Quote, not accept all the items on the Quote, or may add some more items on the Quote. In this case, you may edit the Quote (**Documents**) (**Default** ribbon), before you confirm the Quote and convert it to an Invoice.* |
| --- | --- |


| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *If you have already confirmed a Quote and converted it to an Invoice, and there are still any discrepancies in the Quote, for example the correct quantities of goods accepted, you may edit the Invoice (**Documents**) (**Default** ribbon), before you update (post) the Invoice to the ledger.* |
| --- | --- |


| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *If you did not edit the Invoice (converted Quote), you will then need to process a Credit note to correct the updated (posted) Invoice with the incorrect amounts and quantities.* |
| --- | --- |


| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | *Quotes may also be converted to Invoices on the [**Confirm quotes and orders***](<doc-grid-confirm-quotes.md>) *context menu option on the "Document entry" list screen for the Quotes document type.* |
| --- | --- |


**To confirm and convert a Quote to an Invoice:**

1. On the **Default** ribbon, select **Input → Confirm** (**Shift + F3**).

![Image](<lib/documents-confirm-quotes-options.png>)

2. Select **Quotes** in the "*Document type*" field. The screen will change to reflect Quotes, Invoices and Debtors, and a list of the Quotes.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *By default, only the open and unconfirmed Quotes will be listed. To list the confirmed (Quotes already confirmed and converted to Invoices) select (tick) the "Show posted" field.* |
| --- | --- |


3. Select the Quotes to be confirmed and converted to Invoices. Click on the **Add selected** button. The selected Quotes will be transferred to the "*Quotes to convert to invoice*" section of the screen.
3. Enter or select the Date on which the Quotes was accepted, if necessary.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *By default, the system date is displayed. You may select (tick) the "Use document date" option. The Quotes will be converted to Invoices using the original document date.* |
| --- | --- |


5. Click on the **Convert** button. A confirmation message is displayed:

*&nbsp;"Do you wish to delete all Quotes?"*&nbsp;

6. Click on the **Yes** button to convert the Quotes to Invoices and to delete the Quotes.&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *If you click on the **No** button, the selected Quotes will still be confirmed and converted to Invoices. These Quotes will still be available to edit.*&nbsp; *You may delete the Quotes manually in **Edit → Delete → [Documents***](<delete-documents.md>) *menu on the **Default** ribbon.* |
| --- | --- |


7. The Quotes will be removed from the "*Quote to convert to invoice*" list, and will be ready to be edited in the Invoices document type on the Document list screen.
7. Click on the **Cancel** button, or press the **Esc** key on your keyboard, to close or exit this screen.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | *You may also search for a specific Quote by entering the Quote number, and click on the **Search** button.*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | *You may also select a specific Debtor (customer / client) account by clicking on the **Select debtor** button. The screen will change from the list of available Quotes to the lookup for Debtor (customer / client) accounts. (It also includes Creditor (supplier / vendor) accounts).* ![Image](<lib/documents-confirm-quotes-debtor-select.png>) *Select the Debtor (customer / client) account and click on the **OK** button. The screen will change to the normal screen and the available Quotes will be displayed.* |
| --- | --- |
