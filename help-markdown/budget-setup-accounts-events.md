# Enter budget for special events (Setup - Accounts)

***

Each month can be overwritten so that the budget can be flexed month by month to accommodate seasonal trends and spending patterns of your business. The system will keep a running total for you, so that as you are flexing the monthly budget, you can see the effect on the annual amount.&nbsp; &nbsp;

You may enter budget figures for specific dates and/or periods. For example, if you need to budget 5,000.00 for a special event, on 1 May 2024 - extra sales for a special promotion sale, you may add that.&nbsp;

**To enter budget figures for special events:**

1. On the **Setup** ribbon, select **Setup → Accounts**. ![Image](<lib/budget-setup-acoounts-special-events.png>)
1. Select the “*Sales*” account. On the **Budget** tab, click on the ![Image](<lib/record-icons-05.png>) button to create a new record.
1. The system date will automatically be displayed. Enter or select the date (i.e. “*2024/05/01*”) for the special event.
1. Enter the amount for the&nbsp; budget (i.e. “*–5000*” ) in die “*Amount*” column.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *For a credit amount, insert minus sign “**–**“. The credit amount will be indicated with a minus sign after the amount (e.g. 5,000.00#8202;**–** in the "Amount" column.*&nbsp; *For a debit amount just insert the amount without the minus sign “**–**“. The amount will automatically be divided this amount into 12 months in the "Amount" field.*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


5. Enter the reference (e.g. “*Sale*” ) in the “*Reference*” column.
5. Enter the description (e.g. “*Special promotion*” ) in die “*Description*” column.
5. Click on the ![Image](<lib/record-icons-08.png>) button to save the new budget for “*2024/05/31*”.
5. Click on the **Save** button.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *If you do not click on the **Save** button, any budget figures entered, or changed, will not be saved.*&nbsp; |
