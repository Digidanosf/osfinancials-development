# Creditor accounts - Appointments tab

***

In osFinancials5/TurboCASH5, the "*Creditor accounts - Appointments*" tab allows you to view and manage appointments specifically related to a selected creditor (supplier/vendor) account. This tab provides a centralised view of all scheduled creditor appointments that have been added in the **Calendar** (**Default** ribbon).

By accessing the "*Creditor accounts - Appointments*" tab, you will be able to see a comprehensive list of all creditor appointments/events that have been scheduled in the Calendar. This includes appointments for meetings, discussions, notes, or any other relevant activities associated with your creditor account.
