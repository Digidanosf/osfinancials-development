# Dynareg plugins

***

| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *The **DYNADBREG Plugin** cannot be removed from the **Plugins → Tools** menu, as it is necessary for the functioning of certain features in osFinancials5 and TurboCASH5.* |
| --- | --- |


The Dyna reg Plugins can be accessed from the **Plugins** menu under the **Setup** ribbon, provided they are activated in the **Tools → [Activate plugins**](<plugins-activate.md>) menu. This collection comprises additional plugins for documents, e-commerce, bank import, free fields stock, document layouts, and Free POS (Standard POS). These plugins are available for purchase from our online shop and can be enabled accordingly.

# Activation of Dynareg Plugins

Once you have purchased plugins from our shop, follow these steps to enable them:

1. Navigate to **Plugins → Tools → DYNADBREG**.

![Image](<lib/plugins-dyna-reg-list.png>)

2. Select the desired option and click the **Enable** button. A confirmation message will appear.&nbsp;

*"Add new item?"*

3. Click **OK**. Another confirmation message will appear.&nbsp;

*"Script executed\!"*

4. Click **OK**.
4. Click **Close** and reopen the Set of Books for this plugin to take effect.

For more information and to purchase plugins, visit: [osFinancials Webshop - Small Adjustments](<https://www.osfinancials.org/en/webshop/small-adjusments> "target=\"\_blank\"").
