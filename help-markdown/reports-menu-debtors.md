# Debtors (Reports menu)

***

On the **Reports** ribbon, select **Reports → Debtors**.&nbsp;

&nbsp;![Image](<lib/reports-menu-debtors.png>)

The following reports are available to manage the accounts in the debtor's ledger:

1. **Debtor statement** – Debtor statements and Debtor statements - Outstanding.
1. **Age analysis** - Balances detail, Balances detail history (open item) and Balances reports. These reports offers different options of views to support the details of debtor statements.
1. **Listing** - Contacts, Listing, List delivery address, List postal address or List details, Tax and company registration numbers and the Credit control listing (Credit limit vs current balance and available balances as at the date and time of printing) report.
1. **Print labels** - Postal and delivery address labels.
1. **Transactions** - Transactions - Open item, Transactions - Standard and Transactions - Extra details.
