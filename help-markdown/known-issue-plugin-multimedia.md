![Image](<lib/bug-mutimedia-BATCH type-view-multimedia.png>)

Could possibly mean that the Batch number&nbsp; "***00000001***" does not include a prefix while in many Sets of Books the batch number may include a prefix "***B0000001***" or "***J0000001***"&nbsp;
