# Credit notes - Items (Reports menu)

***

The Credit notes - Items per period report displays the Stock code, Stock description and totals (i.e. Quantities and Selling prices) for each stock item.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *This report only includes Credit notes which are posted and/or confirmed. Unconfirmed (open) Credit notes will be **NOT** be included in this report.* *To view a list of unposted documents, which is not updated to the ledger, go to **Input → [Checking unposted items***](<checking-for-unposted-items.md>) *(**Default** ribbon).* |
| --- | --- |


# Credit notes - Items per period report options

**To print a Credit notes - Items per period report:**&nbsp;

1. On the **Reports** ribbon, select **Reports → Sales → Credit notes → Items**.&nbsp;

![Image](<lib/reports-sales-credit-notes-items-options.png>)

2. Select the period on the **Per period** tab. You may click on the **Free selection** tab to select a specific date or a range of dates.
2. Click on the **OK** button.&nbsp;
