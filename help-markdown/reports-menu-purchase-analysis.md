# Purchase analysis (Reports menu)

***

Various reports are available on the **Reports → Purchase analysis** menu to analyse purchase&nbsp; documents.&nbsp;

Purchase documents per period (Reporting groups):![Image](<lib/reports-purchase-analysis-menu.png>)

Five (5) reporting groups (i.e. Creditor group 1, Creditor group 2, Document group 1, Document group 2 and Salespersons):&nbsp;

1. **Purchases and supplier returns per period** – This will list both the transaction details of posted purchases and supplier returns for a selected period or date(s).
1. **Purchases per period** – This will list the transaction details of posted purchases only for a selected period or date(s).&nbsp;
1. **Supplier returns per period** – This will list the transaction details of posted supplier returns only for a selected period or date(s).&nbsp;
1. **Orders per period** – This will list the transaction details of confirmed (posted) orders only for a selected period or date(s). &nbsp;
1. **Purchases per product - Stock group 1** and **Stock group 2** are also available to analyse the purchases and supplier returns of trading stock items (default stock type).&nbsp;
