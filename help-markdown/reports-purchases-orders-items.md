# Orders - Items (Reports menu)

***

The Orders - Items per period report displays the Stock code, Stock description and totals (i.e. Quantities and Cost prices) for each stock item.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *This report only includes Orders which are posted and/or confirmed. Unconfirmed (open) Orders will be **NOT** be included in this report.* |
| --- | --- |


# Orders - Items per period report options

**To print Orders - Items per period reports:**&nbsp;

1. On the **Reports** ribbon, select **Reports → Purchases → Orders → Items**.&nbsp;

![Image](<lib/reports-purchases-orders-items-options.png>)

2. Select the period on the **Per period** tab. You may click on the **Free selection** tab to select a specific date or a range of dates.
2. Click on the **OK** button.&nbsp;
