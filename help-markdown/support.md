# Support information

This option will provide you with contact information for technical assistance.

**To display osFinancials5 Support information:**&nbsp;

1. On the **Start** ribbon, select **Help → Support**. &nbsp;

![Image](<lib/support.png>)

2. Check the necessary details.
2. Click on the **OK** button, or press the **Enter** key on your keyboard, if you do not wish to link to the Website. You may also press the **Esc** key on your keyboard to exit this screen.

**Support Contract**:

The support contract grants you direct access to our team for issue resolution, subject to a "*fair use*" policy. Support is provided via telephone and/or email. Additionally, you'll receive interim updates of osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 where bugs are fixed and new features introduced.

**Extra Tickets**:

Additional support tickets beyond the "*fair use*" allocation can be purchased at a discounted rate for contract holders.

Please note that development or modification of osFiosFinancials5/TurboCASH5 document layouts are not included in the support contract.
