# Delete accounts

***

You may delete a ledger (income/expense, bank or tax) account if the account has no transactions and/or balances.&nbsp;

You may disable a ledger (income/expense, bank or tax) account to block (prevent) posting of any documents or batch transactions for the ledger (income/expense, bank or tax) account.&nbsp;

**To delete a Ledger (Income/Expense, Bank or Tax) account:**

1. On the **Setup** ribbon, select **Setup → Accounts**.&nbsp;
1. Select the account, and click on the ![Image](<lib/icon-accounts-delete.svg>) **Delete** icon. A confirmation message will be displayed.

*"Do you want to delete?"*&nbsp;

3. Once again, make sure it is absolutely the correct ledger (income/expense, bank or tax) account.&nbsp;
3. Click on the **Yes** button. This will permanently remove the ledger (income/expense, bank or tax) account.&nbsp;

# Disable accounts&nbsp;

**To disable a Ledger (Income/Expense, Bank or Tax) account:**

If a ledger (income/expense, bank or tax) account has transactions and/or balances, you cannot delete it. In the "*Posted*" field, the number of transactions processed (posted) for the selected account will be displayed. Once any transactions have been processed (posted) (the value is more than 0), you will not be allowed to delete the account. &nbsp;

1. On the **Setup** ribbon, select **Setup → Accounts**. &nbsp;
1. Select the account.
1. Select **Disabled** in the "*Status*" field and click on the **Save** button. This will block posting of any documents or batch transactions for the ledger (income / expense, bank or tax) account.&nbsp;
1. Click on the **Save** button.
