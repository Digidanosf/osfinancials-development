# Purchases - Purchases (Reports menu)

***

This option allows you to print the posted Purchase documents per period report. It displays the Stock code, Stock description, Quantities, Selling prices and Cost prices (if applicable to stock items).&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *This report only includes Purchase documents which are posted. Unposted Purchase documents will be **NOT** be included in this report.* |
| --- | --- |


# Purchases per period - Quantities report options

**To print Purchases per period reports:**&nbsp;

1. On the **Reports** ribbon, select **Reports → Purchases → Purchase documents → Purchases**.&nbsp;

![Image](<lib/reports-purchases-purchases-options.png>)

2. Select the period on the **Per period** tab. You may click on the **Free selection** tab to select a specific date or a range of dates.
2. Click on the **OK** button.&nbsp;
