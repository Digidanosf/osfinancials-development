# Contacts

***

The Contacts feature in osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 refer to individuals or entities associated with debtor (customer/client) and creditor (supplier/vendor) accounts. The Contacts feature allows you to maintain a list and details of contacts linked to these accounts for easy reference and communication.

Contacts play a crucial role in processing sales and purchase documents within osFinancials5/TurboCASH5. They help specify the relevant contact person or entity associated with a particular transaction. Here's how contacts are utilised in the process:

1. **Sales documents**: When creating sales documents such as quotes, invoices, and credit notes, the default contact linked to the debtor (customer/client) account will be automatically selected. This ensures that the appropriate contact is associated with the transaction. The contact specified in the sales document represents the person responsible for the transaction or serves as the primary point of contact for the customer.
1. **Purchase documents**: Similarly, when generating purchase documents like orders, purchases and supplier returns, the default contact linked to the creditor (supplier/vendor) account will be automatically selected. This ensures that the relevant contact information is associated with the transaction. The contact specified in the purchase document represents the person responsible for the transaction or serves as the primary point of contact for the supplier.

By assigning the correct contacts to the respective accounts, osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 ensures that the appropriate contact information is readily available when processing sales and purchase transactions. This helps maintain accurate and effective communication with customers, clients, suppliers, and vendors throughout the transactional process.

Contacts will also be printed on document layout files for sales documents such as quotes, invoices, and credit notes as well as purchase documents such as orders, purchases and supplier returns providing additional information for the recipient or reference.

Including contact information on document layout files for sales and purchase documents is a valuable feature in osFinancials5/TurboCASH5. By printing contacts on these documents, you provide additional information for the recipient or reference, enhancing the clarity and professionalism of the transaction. This ensures that the necessary contact details, such as the name, phone number, email address, or any other relevant information, are readily available for easy communication or reference purposes. It allows recipients to quickly reach out to the relevant contact person or entity in case of any queries, clarifications, or further discussions related to the transaction.

In osFinancials5/TurboCASH5, the [**Contacts**](<contacts.md>): feature will list all the contacts, where you can manage and maintain contact information for debtors (customers/clients) and creditors (suppliers/vendors). You may add new contacts, update contact details, delete contacts and print a list of all the contacts in your Set of Books.

Additionally, you may maintain and manage contacts on the following options on the **Default** ribbon.

1. [**Debtors - Contacts**](<debtor-tab-contacts.md>) tab: This tab specifically focuses on managing contacts associated with debtor accounts. You can add, edit, or delete contacts linked to debtor accounts, ensuring that the contact details are up to date.
1. [**Creditors - Contacts**](<creditor-tab-contacts.md>) tab: Similar to the [**Debtors - Contacts**](<debtor-tab-contacts.md>) tab, this tab is dedicated to managing contacts associated with creditor accounts. You can modify the contact information for your suppliers/vendors to maintain accurate records.
1. [**Calendar - Contacts**](<calendar-contacts.md>) feature: provides the ability to manage contacts, ensuring that any changes made to contact information are reflected across the system. The following options on the **Default** ribbon allow you to create, edit, or delete contacts, with all updates being synchronized with the **Contacts** in the Calendar.

By utilising these options, you can conveniently manage contacts within osFinancials5/TurboCASH5, ensuring that all contact information remains consistent and accessible across different sections in a Set of Books.

**To create / add / edit / delete / print contacts:**&nbsp;

1. On the **Default** ribbon, select **Contacts**.&nbsp;
