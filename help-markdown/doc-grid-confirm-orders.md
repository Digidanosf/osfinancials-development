# Context menu - Confirm quotes and orders - Orders

***

Once the creditor (supplier / vendor) delivers the Order, it can then be confirmed and converted to a Purchase. Once a Order is confirmed and converted to a Purchase document, you may need to edit the Purchase, before you update (post) the Purchase to the ledger.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | *Quotes and Orders may also be confirmed in **Input → [Confirm***](<confirm-quotes-orders.md>) *menu on the **Default** ribbon.* *(**Shift+F3**).*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *If the [**Document posting***](<doc-setting-post-delete-buttons.md>) *option is selected (ticked) on the **Documents** tab of **Access control** (**Setup** ribbon), the caption of the **Posting** button, will change to **Confirmation** button if Quotes and Orders are selected as the document type.* |
| --- | --- |


| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *The **Documents →** **Confirm quotes and orders** option on the context menu, is only available, if the Quotes or Orders document type is selected.* |
| --- | --- |


**To confirm Order(s):**

1. Select the "*Order*" document type.
1. Select the Order(s) to confirm.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | *If you need to select multiple Orders, press the **Ctrl** or **Shift** key and click on each Order, you need to confirm.* |
| --- | --- |


![Image](<lib/documents-context-menu-confirm-orders.png>)

3. Right-click and select **Documents →** **Confirm quotes and orders** option on the context menu. A confirmation message is displayed:

*&nbsp;"Do you wish to: (2) Orders Confirmation" (where (2) is the number of documents selected).*

4. Click on the **Yes** button to continue. A confirmation message is displayed:

*&nbsp;"Do you wish to delete this Orders?"*&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *If you click on the **No** button, the selected Orders will still be confirmed and converted to Purchases. These Orders will still be available to edit.*&nbsp; *You may delete the Orders manually in **Edit → [Documents***](<delete-documents.md>) *menu on the **Default** ribbon.* |
| --- | --- |


5. Click on the **Yes** button to continue.&nbsp;
5. The converted Orders will be available will be available as unposted Purchases.&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *You need to check and edit the Messages in the Document header, if necessary.*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | *The source document, Order number (e.g. "Ref:OR000002") will be added to the third Message line in the Document header&nbsp; of the Purchase.* |
| --- | --- |
