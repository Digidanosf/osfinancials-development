# Account groups - Financial categories - Income statement

***

Each of the Income statement Financial categories, which are INCOME and EXPENSES, are further classified into reporting groups for accounts, which are described as follows:

| ***FINANCIAL CATEGORY*** | ***REPORTING GROUPS FOR ACCOUNTS*** | ***DESCRIPTION*** | ***\*DR / CR*** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| ![Image](<lib/admon-material-accounts-retained-income.svg>)*NET PROFIT* | *RETAINED INCOME* | *Income received is greater than incurred expenses.* | ***CREDIT*** |
| ![Image](<lib/admon-material-accounts-retained-income.svg>)*/ NET LOSS* |  | *Incurred expenses are greater than income received.* | ***DEBIT*** |
| ![Image](<lib/admon-material-accounts-income.svg>)*INCOME* | *INCOME*&nbsp; *OTHER INCOME* | *This category includes revenue generated from the sale of goods and services.*&nbsp; *This category includes revenue from ventures and activities outside of the normal business activities.* | ***CREDIT***&nbsp; ***CREDIT*** |
| ![Image](<lib/admon-material-accounts-expenses.svg>)*EXPENSES* | *EXPENSES* | *This category includes expenses incurred in the normal business activities to generate income.* | ***DEBIT*** |


RETAINED INCOME, at the beginning of the financial year, the income/expense accounts are zeroed and the resulting balance transferred to the retained income account.

Income / Expense accounts created (in osFinancials5/TurboCASH5) should be assigned to an appropriate reporting group and linked to a financial category in osFinancials5/TurboCASH5.

![Image](<lib/admon-material-accounts-income.svg>) *INCOME or* ![Image](<lib/admon-material-accounts-expenses.svg>)&nbsp; *EXPENSES*

The Financial results influence the net worth of a business. A profit will increase the capital value of a business; the opposite is true for a loss.

\*The DR/CR column in the table indicates whether the account has a debit or credit balance. The NET PROFIT or NET LOSS is calculated by subtracting the total expenses from the total income. At the beginning of the financial year, the income/expense accounts are zeroed, and the resulting balance is transferred to the RETAINED INCOME account.

Accounts created in osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 should be assigned to an appropriate reporting group and linked to a financial category (INCOME or EXPENSES) in osFinancials5/TurboCASH5. The financial results influence the net worth of a business, with a profit increasing the capital value of a business and a loss decreasing it.
