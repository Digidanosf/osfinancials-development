# What is Registering / Cancellation of Registration for VAT/GST/Sales Tax?&nbsp;

**Registering for VAT/GST/Sales Tax**: Registering for VAT/GST/Sales Tax involves the formal process of enrolling as a tax vendor with the relevant tax authority in your jurisdiction. When your business reaches a specified sales threshold, you are required to collect and remit taxes on behalf of the government. The registration process typically includes providing essential business details, obtaining a unique tax identification number, and understanding the tax rates and regulations applicable to your industry. Registering ensures compliance with tax laws, allows you to collect tax from customers, and entitles you to claim input tax credits.

**Cancellation of Registration for VAT/GST/Sales Tax**: Cancellation of registration for VAT/GST/Sales Tax entails formally informing the tax authority that you will no longer be conducting taxable activities. This could be due to various reasons, such as ceasing business operations or falling below the minimum sales threshold. Upon cancellation, you are no longer required to collect tax from customers or remit it to the government. The process may involve settling any outstanding tax liabilities, updating your accounting records, and adjusting your system settings to reflect the change in your tax status. It's important to follow the proper procedures to avoid any penalties or complications related to tax compliance.

You'll follow the steps to set up your VAT/GST/Sales Tax accounts, configure tax codes and percentages, and customize document layouts to meet your tax jurisdiction's requirements. This ensures accurate tax calculations and proper tax invoicing in your transactions.
1. [**New User in osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 without Tax Registration**](<tax-not-registered-new-osfinanci.md>): If you're new to osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 and not a registered VAT/GST/Sales Tax vendor, this choice is appropriate. You'll need to adjust the system's default settings for tax accounts to reflect your non-registered status. This includes disabling or removing VAT/GST/Sales Tax accounts, configuring layouts to reflect non-taxable transactions, and updating document names if necessary. This option ensures accurate transaction documentation without tax-related elements.
1. [**Existing User Opting for Tax Deregistration**](<tax-deregister-osfinancials.md>): If you're an existing osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 user who has decided to cancel your VAT/GST/Sales Tax registration, or if you have been served a notice of cancellation of registration from your tax authority. Following this route involves steps to update your tax accounts' status to *'Disabled*,' and possibly remove them if unposted transactions are absent. Additionally, you need to process all tax-related transactions until the date of cancellation of registration, ensuring accurate records. After the date of deregistration, it's crucial to adjust your system to process transactions without Tax (VAT/GST/Sales tax) to reflect the updated tax-related changes accurately. Managing this process diligently will help maintain the integrity of your financial records.
