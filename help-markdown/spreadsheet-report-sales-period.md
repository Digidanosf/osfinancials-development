# Spreadsheet - Sales analysis -  Sales per period

***

The Spreadsheet report - Stock - Sales per period will list the total sales amount excluding as well as including tax (VAT/GST/ Sales tax) for each of the selected periods.&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *This report only includes document transactions which are posted. Unposted documents will be **NOT** be included in this report.* *To view a list of unposted documents, which is not updated to the ledger, go to **Input → [Checking unposted items***](<checking-for-unposted-items.md>) *(**Default** ribbon).* |
| --- | --- |


**To import Sales per period into a spreadsheet:**&nbsp;

1. On the **Reports** ribbon, select **Spreadsheet reports → Sales analysis → Sales per period**.&nbsp;
1. On the parameters ("*Params*") screen, select the period and click **OK**. The total sales amount, excluding as well as including tax (VAT/GST/ Sales tax), for each period will be listed in the spreadsheet:

![Image](<lib/spreadsheet-reports-stock-sales-period.png>)

| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | ***Month (Column B)*** *This is the number of the month in the Calendar year, e.g.:* *3 = March*&nbsp; *4 = April* *5 = May, etc.* |
