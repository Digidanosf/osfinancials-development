# [osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 - Business Class](<https://www.osfinancials.org/en/latest-news/636-osfinancials-5-business-en> "target=\"\_blank\"")

After years of development and support for osFinancials and TurboCASH, the current model is no longer working effectively. To address this issue, the team is introducing osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 Business Class a new version with additional features and capabilities.

While osFinancials 4 remains free to use, users seeking more advanced functionality can upgrade to osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 Business Class. This version is available free of charge for users with fewer than 500 transactions or documents. For those with more, a yearly contribution of 50 euros (excluding taxes) or 60 dollars will be required.

For users with existing subscriptions for multi-user or retail POS, the license for osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 Business Class will be included as part of their existing subscription.

### Bank import&nbsp;

| ![Bank import plugin icon](<lib/icon-osf-shop-bank.png>) | ***BankImport Plugin** -*&nbsp; ***Licence** : Included in osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 Subscription.*&nbsp; *Documentation : [Help topic*](<plugin-bank-import.md>)*.* *Documentation : osFinancials website - [Bank import plugin*](<https://www.osfinancials.org/en/plugin-manuals/428-bank-import-plugin> "target=\"\_blank\"") *-*&nbsp; *Documentation : osFinancials website - [Bank Reconciliations Plugin*](<https://www.osfinancials.org/en/plugin-manuals/512-bank-reconciliations-plugin> "target=\"\_blank\"") *-*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


The Bank Import plugin included with the osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 Subscription is a handy tool for managing and processing the transactions in your bank and credit card accounts. You can download electronic bank statement files directly from your online banking services at your bank or financial institution. After that, you can use the Bank Import plugin to import these bank statements into your bank accounts. This process saves you a lot of time and effort as you do not have to manually enter and process the transactions of your bank accounts and credit card accounts in separate Payment journals and Receipt Journals.&nbsp;

Additionally, the Bank Import Plugin has the ability to use bank references to automatically assign transactions to the appropriate accounts, This automation not only saves time, but also reduces the risk of human error associated with manual data entry. Overall, using this Bank Import plugin is a smart choice for businesses or individuals who want to optimize their financial workflow within osFinancials5/TurboCASH5.

**Support Contract**:

The support contract grants you direct access to our team for issue resolution, subject to a "*fair use*" policy. Support is provided via telephone and/or email. Additionally, you'll receive interim updates of osFinancials where bugs are fixed and new features introduced.

**Extra Tickets**:

Additional support tickets beyond the "*fair use*" allocation can be purchased at a discounted rate for contract holders.
