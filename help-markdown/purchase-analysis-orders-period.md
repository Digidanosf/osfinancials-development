# Orders per period (Reports menu)

***

The following five (5) reports are available to analyse orders:

1. **Orders per period - Creditor group 1** - List the orders by creditor (supplier / vendor) reporting group 1 linked to creditor (supplier / vendor) accounts on **Creditors** (**Default** ribbon). &nbsp;
1. **Orders per period - Creditor group 2** - List the orders by creditor (supplier / vendor) reporting group 2 linked to creditor (supplier / vendor) accounts on **Creditors** (**Default** ribbon). &nbsp;
1. **Orders per period - Document group 1** - List the orders by document reporting group 1. &nbsp;
1. **Orders per period - Document group 2** - List the orders by document reporting group 2. &nbsp;
1. **Orders per period - Salesperson** - List the orders by salesperson (person who placed or processed the order).&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *This report only includes Orders which are posted and/or confirmed. Unconfirmed (open) Orders will be **NOT** be included in this report.* |

# Purchase analysis - Orders per period report options

**To print Orders by Reporting groups reports:**&nbsp;

1. On the **Reports** ribbon, select **Reports → Purchase analysis → Orders per period**.&nbsp;

![Image](<lib/reports-purchase-analysis-orders-options.png>)

2. Select the following:
1) &nbsp;

   1) **Report** - Select the Orders per period - Creditor group 1 (default), Orders per period - Creditor group 2, Orders per period - Document group 1, Orders per period - Document group 2 or Orders per period - Salesperson report.&nbsp;
   1) **Period** - Select the period on the **Per period** tab. You may click on the **Free selection** tab to select a specific date or a range of dates.
   1) **Sequence** - Select "*Creditor code*" to print the report by account code or "*Description*" to print the report by account description (name).
   1) **New page per account** - If this option is selected (ticked), it will print the transactions for each account on a new page.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *The Company details will not be printed as in the Page header. The company name is displayed in the Page footer.* |
| --- | --- |


3. Click on the **OK** button.&nbsp;
