# Checking for unposted items

***

Before printing reports, you should check for unposted batches and documents. The transactions in batches and documents not posted (updated) to the ledger are not included in the reports.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *Some examples, where this is important:* *Before completing your tax returns* *Before sending out [Debtor statements*](<debtor-statement.md>)*, [Creditor remittance advises*](<creditor-remittance-advices.md>)*,, etc.*&nbsp; &nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | *You may also print a trial balance, income statement or balance sheet for unposted transactions in batches in the [Ledger analyser - Unposted batches*](<ledger-analyser-unposted-reports.md>)*.* |
| --- | --- |


**To check for unposted items:**&nbsp;

1. On the **Default** ribbon, select **Input →** **Checking unposted items**&nbsp;
1. This will list the unposted batches and the number of documents for each document type (i.e. invoices, credit notes, purchases and supplier returns). &nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *If there are no unposted batches and/or documents, this screen will not be displayed.* |
