# Exit osFinancials5

***

| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *It is recommended that you create regular backups of your data before exiting osFinancials5/TurboCASH5. The decision of whether or not to make a backup depends on the amount of data that you have entered.* |
| --- | --- |


**To exit (close) osFinancials5/TurboCASH5:**

1. Click on the&nbsp; ![Image](<lib/icons-screens-close.png>) button on the top right-hand corner of the osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 title bar.&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | *You may also press the **Alt** and **Q** keys.* |
| --- | --- |


| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *If no Set of Books are opened, the osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 program will be closed.*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


2. osFinancials5 will automatically close any Set of Books that is open. The following information message will be displayed:

*osFinancials5 would like to remind you that:*\
*It is good practice to make regular backups and archive copies of your Sets of Books to prevent loss of data.*\
*Do you wish to close osFinancials5?*

3. Take note of this information, and click on the **Yes** button, only if you are absolutely sure that you wish to exit (close) osFinancials5/TurboCASH5.&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *It is recommended that you Backup the Set of Books and store it in a safe place.*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *If you click on the **No** button, osFinancials5 will not close. You may then perform the necessary backups e.g. to a different location before exiting (closing) the program. You may then close the Set of Books and exit osFinancials5/TurboCASH5.*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |
