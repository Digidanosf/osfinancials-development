# Print a Trial Balance for Opening Balances

It is good practice to print a Trial balance in osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 at this point and check it with the Trail balance of your previous accounting records from which you have entered your transactions.&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | *It is recommended that this printed Trial balance be retained with your working papers and the previous trial balance for record and audit purposes.* |
| --- | --- |


**To print a trial balance from the Ledger analyser**:

1. On the **Reports** ribbon, select Ledger analyser 1 or Ledger analyser 2.

![Image](<lib/opening-balance-trial-balance-options 1.png>)

| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *If you need to check the individual balances of the Debtor's accounts in the Debtor's control account (G700), you need to generate Debtor's age analysis report.* *If you need to check the individual balances of the Creditor's accounts in the Creditor's control account (G900), you need to generate Creditor's age analysis report.* *If you need to check the opening balances of the individual stock items in the Stock control account, you need to generate a Stock item listing report.*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


2. Select the following:

   1. **Periods** - On the default “*Per period*” tab, select “*Year*” and Period “*2024/03/01-2025/02/28*”.
   1. **Report type** -Select “*Trial balance*”.
   1. **Reporting group** - The following options are available:

      1. **None** - Select this option. This will list the accounts according to the Account code (account number).
      1. **Reporting group 1** - This will list the accounts according to the Account Group 1 (Groups - Account group1) (Setup ribbon).&nbsp;
      1. **Reporting group 2** - This will list the accounts according to the Account Group 2 (Groups (Account group2) (Setup ribbon).&nbsp;

   1. **Ignore zero balances** - Tick will list only those accounts for which opening balances was entered.
   1. **Viewer Debtors / Creditors** - If this option is not ticked, it will list only the balances of the Debtor's control account and the Creditor's control account.&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | *Tick this option to show the break down of the Debtor accounts in the Debtor's control account and the break down of the Creditor accounts in the Creditor's control account.* ![Image](<lib/trial-balance-view-debtor-creditor.png>) |
| --- | --- |


1. &nbsp;
   6. Include opening balances – Tick this option to include the opening balances.
   6. Opening balances only – Tick this option to include only the opening balances.&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *If any transactions is posted for the selected period, you need to tick this option to list only the accounts for which opening balances have been entered.*&nbsp; *You may leave this option blank, if no other transactions are entered and posted for the selected period.*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


3. Click on the **Report** button. You may change the sequences, and click on the **Print** button to print the trial balance.&nbsp;

An example of the “*Trial balance*” (account code sequence), is as follows:

![Image](<lib/opening-balance-unposted-trial-balance-print.png>)

| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *The totals should balance with the previous financial year. Check that the totals are in balance and that the correct balances correspond with the balances on your source trial balance.* |
