| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *If you click on the **Open** button on the Set of Books screen, and the start of the financial year (for the Set of Books) is older than the system date, the similar confirmation message will be displayed.* *If you click on the **Yes** button, you may proceed to do the year end.*&nbsp; *If you click on the **No** button, you may go to the **Do year end** menu on the **Setup** ribbon.* |

This option will perform year-end processing for you. It will balance all the income/expense accounts transferring the balance of each account to the retained earnings or retained income account.&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *This account will then show your net profit or loss for the year. The balances of the balance sheet accounts are brought forward to the New year.*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


The reporting dates are updated to cater for the New Year. Once the year-end procedure is completed, you will be able to post transactions to your new financial year.&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 stores the transactions and balances for accounts for up to two financial years (This Year and Last Year). You will still be able to view the transactions and reports for the previous closed financial year.*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


| ![Image](<lib/admon-warning.png>) | *Before you may perform this year-end procedure to close off all your accounts for a specific financial year you need to:*&nbsp; *Post any batches (journals) and update any documents to the ledger.*&nbsp; *Do any corrections, if any errors are found and do the year-end adjustments.*&nbsp; *Print the necessary reports and retain it with the necessary supporting documents for record and audit purposes.*&nbsp; *Do a Data integrity check to check that all your data is correct and in balance.*&nbsp; *Make a Backup of the Set of Books.*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *It is recommended that you Disable your Screen Saver in the Windows Operating System, before starting this process. This will prevent the Screen Saver being activated while the process is running.*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


**To do a year end:**&nbsp;

1. On the **Setup** ribbon, select **Do year end**. The following information screen is displayed:&nbsp;

![Image](<lib/year-end-confirm-02.png>)

2. If you are absolutely sure, tick the "*I have read this instruction and understand the action\!*" field. The **Yes** and **No** buttons will be available on this screen.

![Image](<lib/year-end-confirm-03.png>)

| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *If you have not made backups, click on the **No** button and backup your Set of Books.*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


3. If you click on the **Yes** button, a confirmation message is displayed:

![Image](<lib/year-end-confirm-04.png>)

4. Click on the **Yes** button, if you are absolutely sure you have made backups.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-warning.png>) | *This is your last chance to cancel this process. If you want to abort the process, click on the **Yes** button.*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


5. Click on the **No** button to start the process.&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *It is recommended that you do not use your system for other tasks while this process is running.*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


6. Once the process is finished, the confirmation message is displayed:

![Image](<lib/year-end-confirm-05.png>)

7. Click on the **OK** button. You may continue to work in the new year.&nbsp;

# Post to last year&nbsp;

1. It is possible to post transactions in batches (journals) and/or documents (invoices, credit notes, purchases and supplier returns) to the previous financial year. For example, if transactions in a batch includes a date that corresponds to the previous financial year (for instance, if the current financial year spans from 01/03/2022 to 28/02/2023 and the transaction date is 10/03/2021), a confirmation message will appear asking if you want to proceed with posting the batch. The message will state:&nbsp;

*"Do you want to continue posting? Batch contains transactions posting to last year\!"*

2. Check that the date is correct - Last year's date.
2. Click on the **Yes** button. osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 *w*ill post the transaction to the previous (last) financial year.

**If the date(s) of the transactions is incorrect:**&nbsp;

1. Click on the **No** button.
1. Correct the date(s) of the transaction in the batch to this year.
1. Balance the batch once again.
1. Post the batch.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *It is also a good idea to restrict posting to the previous financial year. You may do this by selecting **Setup → Reporting dates** (**Setup** ribbon) and remove the tick in the "Post to last year" field.*&nbsp; *Although you will not be able to post transactions to that financial year, you may still print reports. You may at any time select this field again should you wish to post to the previous financial year.*&nbsp; |

# Do a year end in the future

Should you initiate the year end process (**Do year end** (**Setup** ribbon)) and the financial year ends in the future, a similar confirmation message to the following, will be displayed:

![Image](<lib/year-end-confirm-06.png>)

If you do not wish to proceed at this stage, click **Yes**.
