# Stock items - Context menu

***

Stock item context menu options:

* [Print single item](<stock-print-single-item.md>) - You may select reports from the **Stock items** (**Default** ribbon) to print all stock items or selected stock item(s):
* [Copy / Pate](<stock-context-copy-paste.md>) - You may copy the specific data of a selected stock item and paste (replace) the data of selected stock item(s).&nbsp;
* [Reporting Stock group 1 / 2](<stock-context-groups-change.md>) - You may change the Stock group 1 and/or 2&nbsp; for selected stock item(s).&nbsp;
* [Mass check](<stock-mass-check-items.md>) - This option will run a check on all stock items.&nbsp;
