# Edit Bank accounts

***

You may have more than one Bank account and wish to add this to your Chart of accounts. osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 will have set up a Bank account for you in your selected template. (In this example, account number “*810-000 - Current Bank account*” is used).

Examples of cash books (bank accounts) in osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 could include:

* **Checking account** - This cash book represents a primary bank account used for daily transactions and general banking activities.
* **Savings account** - This cash book represents a bank account specifically designated for savings and accumulation of funds.
* **Petty cash** - This cash book is used to track and manage small cash transactions and expenses within an organization.
* **Credit card account** - This cash book represents a bank account associated with a credit card, used for tracking credit card transactions and payments.
* **Loan account** - This cash book represents a bank account associated with a loan, used for tracking loan payments and balances.
* **Investment account** - This cash book represents a bank account used for managing investments, such as stocks, bonds, or mutual funds.
* **Foreign currency account** - This cash book represents a bank account denominated in a foreign currency, used for managing transactions and balances in that currency.
* **Escrow account** - This cash book represents a bank account used for holding funds in trust for specific purposes, such as real estate transactions or legal settlements.
* **Trust account** - This cash book represents a bank account used for holding funds in trust on behalf of clients or beneficiaries.
* **Expense reimbursement account** - This cash book represents a bank account used for tracking and reimbursing employee expenses.

These are just a few examples, and the specific cash books will depend on the needs and requirements of your organisation.

If you wish to change the Bank account description and batches name you my edit the account and batch types.

**To change a Bank account:**

1. On the **Setup** ribbon, select **Setup → Accounts**. The *Accounts* screen; listing your available accounts will be displayed.&nbsp;

2) Select the “*810-000 - Current Bank account*” ("*Current assets" – Reporting group*).

![Image](<lib/accounts-setup-bank.png>)

2. In the "*Description*" field, replace “*Current bank account*” with the new Bank account description e.g. “*ABC-Bank*”.
2. Click on the **Bank** tab.&nbsp;
2. Enter the unique name for the Payments and Receipts batches, e.g. “*ABC-Payments*” and the “*ABC-Receipts*”. This will automatically add (create) the batch types which may be edited in **Setup → Batch types**.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-plugin.png>) | [***Bank import plugin***](<plugin-bank-import.md>) *- Is included in the osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 Subscription - [Bank Reconciliations Plugin Manual*](<https://www.osfinancials.org/en/plugin-manuals/512-bank-reconciliations-plugin> "target=\"\_blank\"") *- [BankImport Plugin Manual*](<https://www.osfinancials.org/en/plugin-manuals/428-bank-import-plugin> "target=\"\_blank\"") *-*&nbsp; *If the&nbsp; “Default bank reconciliation method” option is not selected (tick removed) on the **Setup → System parameters** (**Setup** ribbon#8202;**)**; the Payments and Receipts batch fields will be combined. You may then select either the Payments journal or the Receipts journal.*&nbsp; ![Image](<lib/accounts-setup-bank-tab-bank-import.png>) |
| --- | --- |


5. Click on the **Save** button.
