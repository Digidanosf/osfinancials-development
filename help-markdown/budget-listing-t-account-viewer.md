# Budget listing - T-Account viewer

***

You may list the Budget figures (for all accounts or specific accounts), as entered or processed in the **Budgets** (**Reports** ribbon); or on **Setup → Accounts** (**Setup** ribbon), in the [**T-Account viewer**](<t-account-viewer.md>). You may print these budget figures and/or export the budget figures in [**T-Account viewer**](<t-account-export-transactions.md>) to a spreadsheet.

**To print a list of budgets in the T-Account viewer:**

1. If the **Budget** option is selected on the "*Trial balance, Income statement",* or "*Balance sheet*" report types in the Ledger analyser 1 or 2, you may right-click and select the **Show details** option. This will display the budget figures in the T-Account viewer.&nbsp;

![Image](<lib/budget-t-account.png>)

2. Select the dates from and to to include in the budget figures, if necessary.&nbsp;

## | ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *The Batch number is displayed as "0", to indicate that this is a budget listing.* |
| --- | --- |


## | ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *The "Show contra" option will not list the Contra accounts, since no Contra accounts is available for budgets.* |
| --- | --- |


3. Click on the **Print** button. You may also click on the **Export** button to export the budget figures to a spreadsheet.

![Image](<lib/budget-t-account-print.png>)
