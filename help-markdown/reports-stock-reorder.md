# Stock - Reorder (Reports menu)

***

You may print the following two (2) reports to manage the reorder quantities an stock levels of your stock items.&nbsp;

1. **Reorder report** - Displays the only those stock items which are due to order.&nbsp;
1. **Current stock levels** - Displays all stock item quantities.&nbsp;
