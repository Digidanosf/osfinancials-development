# Adjust stock tab

***

The **Adjust stock** tab on the on the Stock item list screen will only be available when the mode in set to Default. In the **Advanced mode** is not selected, the **Adjust stock** tab will not be displayed.&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | *The stock item screen may also be launched as a floating screen from the **Input → [Adjust stock***](<stock-adjustments-input-menu.md>) *menu on the **Default** ribbon.* |
| --- | --- |


![Image](<lib/stock-list-tabs-adjust-stock.png>)

**To adjust selling prices for all stock items:**

1. Select the first stock item from the list.&nbsp;
1. The percentage in the "*Use percentage*" field, is inactive.
1. Use transactions - To be advised.
1. Enter or select the date ("*Date*" field).&nbsp;
1. Click on the **OK** button. All selling prices will automatically be adjusted by the specified percentage.&nbsp;

**To adjust specified selling prices for stock items:**

1. Select the stock item from the list.&nbsp;
1. Enter the selling prices 1 / 2 and 3 in the "*Exclusive*" field.
1. Enter or select the date ("*Date*" field).&nbsp;
1. Click on the **\<-Set selected to this price** button. A confirmation message will be displayed.&nbsp;
