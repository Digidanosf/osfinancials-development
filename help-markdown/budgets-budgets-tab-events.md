# Budgets - Enter budget figures for special events

***

In this example, on 1 May 2024 we need to budget 5,000.00 extra sales for a special promotion. You may enter budget figures for specific dates and/or periods to accommodate, for example, special events, fluctuations and business patterns.

**To enter budget figures for special events:**&nbsp;

1. On the **Reports** ribbon, select **Budgets**.

![Image](<lib/budget-03.png>)

2. **Period** – Select the period “*Year*” (i.e. “*2024/03/01*” to “*2025/02/28*") to enter your budget figures for the selected period.
2. Click on the **Refresh** button. This will list all the existing ledger accounts in your “*Chart of accounts*” (existing as added, edited in the **Setup → Accounts** (**Setup** ribbon)), in the Account code sequence. &nbsp;
2. Select the “*Sales*” account and the month “*2024/05/01*” - May 2024.&nbsp;
2. Reference – The default or previous reference entered, will be displayed, for example, “*BUDG*”. Enter “*BUDGET SALE“* to indicate that it is a sales event that is scheduled for May 2024; or that took place in May 2024.
2. **Description** – The description as previously entered, is “*Budget 2024/2025*”.&nbsp; Enter “*Budget 2024/2025 – Sale*”.&nbsp;
2. Only enter the additional amount with which&nbsp; you need to increase the budget for May 2024.&nbsp;
2. To increase the existing amount for May 2024 from –10,000 to –15000, only enter&nbsp; –5000 in the amount field (before the **Apply to selection** button).

10. Click **Yes** to save the increased budget figure for May 2024.
