## Pivot view of Activa transactions

You may use the [Pivot](<ledger-analyser-pivot.md>) feature [Ledger analyser](<ledger-analyser.md>), to filter for Fixed assets and Depreciation transactions on the following report types.

* [Trail balance](<ledger-analyser-trial-balance.md>) -&nbsp; Fixed assets main accounts (Fixed assets at cost and/or Fixed assets accumulated depreciation sub- accounts} and the Depreciation - Income expense account type.
* [Balance sheet](<ledger-analyser-balance-sheet.md>) - Fixed assets main accounts (Fixed assets at cost and/or Fixed assets accumulated depreciation sub- accounts).
* [Income statement](<ledger-analyser-income-statement.md>) - Depreciation - Income expense account type.
* [Standard column balances](<ledger-analyser-standard-ccolumn.md>) - Fixed assets main accounts (Fixed assets at cost and/or Fixed assets accumulated depreciation sub- accounts} and the Depreciation - Income expense account type.
* [This year vs last year](<ledger-analyser-this-last-year.md>) - Fixed assets main accounts (Fixed assets at cost and/or Fixed assets accumulated depreciation sub- accounts} and the Depreciation - Income expense account type.
* [Budget vs actual](<ledger-analyser-budget-actual.md>) - Fixed assets main accounts (Fixed assets at cost and/or Fixed assets accumulated depreciation sub- accounts} and the Depreciation - Income expense account type.
