# Bank charges and items on the bank statement

***

You would only know what your bank charges are when you actually receive your bank statement from the bank. Any bank charges that have to be paid must be entered into your "*Payments journal*", and only interest received must be entered into your "*Receipts journal*".

It is always a good idea to use a reference number; such as your Bank Statement number; so as have a reference to the specific bank statement.&nbsp;

You now need to go back to your Payments journal and enter your bank charges. Press the **Esc** key to exit the reconciliation.&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *Before exiting the batch, make sure that you have clicked on the **Apply reconciled** option on the **Process** button to save the “ticks” and reconcile the ticked items).* |
| --- | --- |


**To enter bank charges and finalise the bank reconciliation:**

1. Click on the **Batch entry** icon. (You may also press the **F2** key). The "*Batch type"* screen will be displayed.
1. Select the **Payments Jnl** and click on the **Open** button. The "*Payments journal"* screen will be displayed.
1. Enter the Alias (BatchName) as *“ABC-Payments-BS5*”.
1. Enter the following transaction:

   1) Reference: BS5
   1) Date: 2024/03/31
   1) Description: Bank charges- Bank statement no.5
   1) Account: Bank Charges
   1) Tax: Select No Tax for the purposes of this documentation.&nbsp;
