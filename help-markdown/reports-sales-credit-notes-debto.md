# Credit notes - Debtors (Reports menu)

***

The Debtor - Credit notes per period - Quantities report displays the Stock code, Stock description, Quantities, Selling prices and Cost prices (if applicable to stock items) for each debtor (customer / client) account.&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *This report only includes Credit notes which are posted. Unposted Credit notes will be **NOT** be included in this report.* *To view a list of unposted documents, which is not updated to the ledger, go to **Input → [Checking unposted items***](<checking-for-unposted-items.md>) *(**Default** ribbon).* |
| --- | --- |


# Debtors - Credit notes per period - Quantities report options

**To print a Debtor - Credit notes per period - Quantities report:**&nbsp;

1. On the **Reports** ribbon, select **Reports → Sales → Credit notes → Debtors**.&nbsp;

![Image](<lib/reports-sales-credit-notes-debtors-options.png>)

2. Select the period on the **Per period** tab. You may click on the **Free selection** tab to select a specific date or a range of dates.

3) Click on the **OK** button.&nbsp;
