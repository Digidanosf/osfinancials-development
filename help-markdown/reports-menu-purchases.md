# Purchases (Reports menu)

***

Various reports are available on the **Reports → Purchases**&nbsp; menu to analyse each of the sales documents (i.e. Purchases, Supplier returns and Orders). The reports for Purchases are as follows:![Image](<lib/reports-purchases-menu.png>)

1. **Creditors** – Creditor – Purchases per period – Quantities&nbsp;
1. **Items**&nbsp; - Items per period.&nbsp;
1. **Purchases** – Purchases per period – Quantities.
