# Calculator

***

The Calculator is not available on the **Setup** ribbon (*Tools*) in osFinancials5/TurboCASH5. The calculator is integrated on specific amount fields, on which you may perform certain calculations and transfer the results into the amount fields.

**To access the Calculator:**

1. The Calculator's buttons may also be launched from the Credit limit and Charge amount fields of the **Accounting information** tab of the [Debtor](<debtor-tab-accounting-info.md>) / [Creditor](<creditor-tab-accounting-info.md>) accounts.

![Image](<lib/calculator-amount-fields.png>)

| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | *When focus on certain amount fields, you may press the **Ctrl + F4**) keys to launch the Calculator.*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


2. You may do your calculations. Once a calculation has been completed, the result is transferred to the input field.
