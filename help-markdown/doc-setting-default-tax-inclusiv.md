# Default inclusive for documents

***

By default, the amounts are entered (if manually over-typed) Exclusive of Tax (VAT/GST/Sales Tax) on the amount column of the document lines. To enter and view amounts Inclusive of Tax (VAT/GST/Sales Tax), select (tick) this option.
