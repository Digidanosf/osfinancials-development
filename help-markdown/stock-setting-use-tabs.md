# Use tabs for stock

***

By default, the Stock item screen for a selected stock item, will display the Ledger, Document groups, Image, Multi Groups and Backorders tabs below the Stock information section. &nbsp;

![Image](<lib/stock-use-tabs-default.png>)

**To change the interface of the Stock item screen:**

1. On the **Setup** ribbon, select **Stock information**.
1. On the "*Stock information*" screen, deselect (remove the tick) in the "*Use tabs in stock*" field.

![Image](<lib/stock-use-tabs-setting.png>)

3. Click on the **OK** button. The Stock item screen for a selected Stock item will display a Data tab on which you may click + to expand (or - to collapse) the Stock information (Stock main form), Accounting (Ledger tab) and the Multigroup tabs. change as follows:&nbsp;

![Image](<lib/stock-use-tabs.png>)

| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *You may need to go to the **Tools → [Close active forms***](<close-active-forms.md>) *(**Setup** ribbon), to close the Stock item screen before the changes will take effect.*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |
