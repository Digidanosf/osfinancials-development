# Introduction to the Subscriptions plugin

Subscriptions plugin is developed for those businesses which sell subscriptions, e.g. magazines, newspapers, membership fees, gymnasiums, etc. This Subscriptions plugin could also be used for rental industries, renting items to their customers on for example a monthly basis.

With this plugin you'll never forget to charge your members for a subscription.\
\
You can define for each member:

* Which product or service they bought.
* The repeating frequency (x times per day, week, month, etc.).
* A warning period in which you get automatically reminded to send the customer a notice for continuing using your services (i.e. 31 days before the invoicing date).\
So you'll always get reminded to remind your customer to renew his/her subscriptions.
* The quantity of days before or after the renewal date, allows you to create the invoice(s) by a click on a button.
* A different contract period (i.e. 1 year) with the invoicing period (i.e. per month).

You also can:

* See when the next renewal date will be.
* See when the last invoiced date was.
* Send an automated invoice by email.
* Search through the subscriptions for a specific product/service and see who has a subscription to it.

The number of days before or after the renewal date osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 let you create the invoice with the click on a button. A different contract period (i.e. 1 year) with the invoicing period (i.e. per month).

# Overview to use the Subscriptions plugin

To use the Subscriptions plugin, follow these basic steps:

* [**Activate Subscriptions**](<plugin-subscriptions-activate.md>):

  * Enable the plugin as global settings.
  * Configure default settings for invoice periods, contract periods, and other global plugin options.

* [**Setup Subscriptions**](<plugin-subscriptions-setup.md>):

  * Manage subscription stock items (products).
  * &nbsp;Assign subscriptions to debtor (customer/client) accounts.

* **Process Subscriptions**:

  * [Without Email](<plugin-subscriptions-process.md>): Process warnings and subscriptions without sending email notifications.
  * [With Email](<plugin-subscriptions-email-warni.md>): Process warnings and subscriptions, including sending email notifications.

* [**Processed subscriptions - Invoices and Quotes**](<process-subscriptions-invoices-q.md>):&nbsp;

  * Edit and print process invoices.&nbsp;
  * Edit and print process quotes.&nbsp;

* [**Generate Subscriptions Reports**](<plugin-subscriptions-reports.md>):

  * Print reports to manage subscriptions for all debtor (customer/client) accounts.

* [**Copy and Paste Subscriptions**](<plugin-subscriptions-copy-paste.md>):

  * &nbsp;Duplicate subscriptions from one debtor account to another.

* [**Subscriptions - Search options**](<plugin-subscriptions-search.md>): You may filter and search for specific documents (invoices an quotes) related to subscriptions.
