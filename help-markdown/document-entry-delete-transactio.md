# Delete all transactions and comments - Documents

***

If you have entered transactions and/or comments in a Document entry screen, you may wish to delete all the transactions and comments, if any, in the document.&nbsp;

**To delete all the transactions and comments:**&nbsp;

1. Click on the **F10:Delete all** icon. A confirmation message is displayed:&nbsp;

*&nbsp;Are you sure you want to clear all the entries in this Invoice?*

2. Click on the **Yes** button, if you are absolutely sure. All the transactions and comments will be removed and deleted from this document.&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-warning.png>) | *If you have clicked on the **F10:Delete all** icon by accident; or if you want to delete only a few transactions and/or comments, click on the **No** button. You may then click on the **F12:\^D:Delete** icon, or press the **F12** key or the **Ctrl** + **D** keys, to delete only the selected lines or rows.* |
