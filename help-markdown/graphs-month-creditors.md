# Graph - Creditors  per month

***

The Graph - Creditors per month displays the debit and credit transaction totals of all creditor (supplier / vendor) accounts per month in a bar chart.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *This graph only includes batch and document transactions which are posted. Unposted batches and documents will be **NOT** be included in this graph.* *To view a list of unposted batches and documents, which is not updated to the ledger, go to **Input → [Checking unposted items***](<checking-for-unposted-items.md>) *(**Default** ribbon).* |
| --- | --- |


# Graph - Creditors per month options

**To print a Graph - Creditors per month:**&nbsp;

1. On the **Reports** ribbon, select **User reports → Graphs → Graphs per month → Creditors**. &nbsp;

![Image](<lib/graphs-creditor-per-month-options.png>)

2. Select the **Period** - Select a period on the **Per period** tab. You may click on the **Free selection** tab to select a specific date or a range of dates to include in the graph.&nbsp;
2. Click on the **OK** button.&nbsp;

# Printed : Graph - Creditors per month&nbsp;

An example of the "*Graph - Creditors per month*", is as follows:

![Image](<lib/graphs-creditor-per-month-print.png>)

| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *The Outstanding amount should balance with the Creditor's control account on all reports, if the same periods are selected. Opening balances will be excluded from this graph.* |
| --- | --- |


The details are as follows:

1. **Vertical Axis** -&nbsp;
1) &nbsp;

   1) **Negative amounts** - Purchase documents and credit batch transactions (e.g. take on opening balances, adjustments, refunds (payments received from creditors), Generate multiple transactions (i.e. Creditors with charge amount), etc. per month. &nbsp; &nbsp;
   1) **Positive amounts** - Supplier returns, payments and other debit transactions (e.g. adjustments, etc. per month).&nbsp;

2. **Horizontal Axis** - This is the Year and Month ("*YYYYMM*" format) in the calendar year.&nbsp;
2. **Totals**&nbsp;
1) &nbsp;

   1) **Purchases/Credit Transactions** - The total amount value of Purchase documents and credit transactions for the entire period.
   1) **Payments/Supplier returns/Debit Transactions** - The total amount value of Supplier return documents and debit transactions for the entire period.
   1) **Outstanding** - This is the difference (movement) of the debit and credit transactions. A negative figure indicates that there are more Purchases and credit transactions than Supplier returns, payments and debit transactions for the period.&nbsp; &nbsp;
