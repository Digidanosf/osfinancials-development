[**F8:Discount**](<documents-discount.md>) icon. The "*Enter overall discount"* does not change discount in all lines - need to enter them manually on next lines&nbsp;

**Normal percentage**

![Image](<lib/discount-percentage-positive.png>)

![Image](<lib/discount-percentage-negative.png>)
