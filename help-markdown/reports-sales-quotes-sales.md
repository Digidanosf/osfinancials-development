# Quotes - Sales (Reports menu)

***

This option allows you to print the posted Quotes per period report. It displays the Stock code, Stock description, Quantities, Selling prices and Cost prices (if applicable to stock items).&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *This report only includes Quotes which are posted and/or confirmed. Unconfirmed (open) Quotes will be **NOT** be included in this report.* |
| --- | --- |


# Quotes per period - Quantities report options

**To print Quotes per period reports:**&nbsp;

1. On the **Reports** ribbon, select **Reports → Sales → Quotes → Sales**.&nbsp;

![Image](<lib/reports-sales-quotes-sales-options.png>)

2. Select the period on the **Per period** tab. You may click on the **Free selection** tab to select a specific date or a range of dates.
2. Click on the **OK** button.&nbsp;
