# Creditor accounts - Contacts tab

***

You may add, delete or enter contact persons for the creditor (supplier / vendor). A list of all contact persons linked to the selected creditor (supplier / vendor) account, you have created, edited or deleted, in the following options on the **Default** ribbon:

* [**Calendar**](<calendar-contacts.md>) &nbsp;
* [**Contacts**](<contacts.md>) &nbsp;

![Image](<lib/creditors-tab-contacts-01.png>)

This will display the First name, Name, Address, Address 2, Address 3, Phone 1, Phone 2, Fax and E-mail address. &nbsp;

The options are as follows:&nbsp;

**New** - This will open a blank contactperson screen.&nbsp;

**Edit** - This will open the selected contact's details screens.&nbsp;

**Delete** - Delete a selected contact.&nbsp;

**Search** - This will initiate a similar screen as in the [**Contacts**](<contacts.md>) (**Default** ribbon) listing all contacts. You can then select a specific contact. You can also use the “*Auto-copy info*” option to copy the details of a selected contact.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *Deleted contact persons will be removed from this list.* |
