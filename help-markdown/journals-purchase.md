# Purchase journal / Purchase returns journal

***

**Purchases Journal**: Used for recording supplier invoices and credit notes received from external suppliers.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | ***Purchase journal for Credit notes received from external suppliers*** *When processing credit notes received from external suppliers in the Purchase Journal, the opposite entry is required to offset the original transaction. In osFinancials5/TurboCASH5, specifically in the **Batch entry** mode, you would enter the amount preceded by a negative sign (minus sign) to transfer the amount to the debit column of the purchase journal.* *By using a negative sign before the amount, the credit note received from external suppliers is treated as a deduction or reduction from the creditor or supplier's account payable. This helps offset the original purchase or invoice and reflects the appropriate adjustment in the financial records.* *Entering the credit note amount in the debit column of the purchase journal ensures accurate recording and reporting of the transaction. It helps maintain proper documentation and provides a clear audit trail of the credit note received from external suppliers.* *Remember to apply the necessary adjustments in the purchase journal to accurately reflect the credit note transaction and its impact on the creditor or supplier's account and overall financial records. This ensures consistency and accuracy in financial reporting and facilitates proper analysis and reconciliation of supplier accounts.* |
