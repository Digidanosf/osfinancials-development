# Orders - Creditors (Reports menu)

***

The Creditor - Orders per period - Quantities report displays the Stock code, Stock description, Quantities, Cost prices for each creditor (supplier / vendor) account.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *This report only includes Orders which are posted and/or confirmed. Unconfirmed (open) Orders will be **NOT** be included in this report.* |
| --- | --- |


# Creditor - Orders per period - Quantities report options

**To print a Creditor - Orders per period - Quantities report:**&nbsp;

1. On the **Reports** ribbon, select **Reports → Purchases → Orders → Creditor**.&nbsp;

![Image](<lib/reports-purchases-orders-creditor-options.png>)

2. Select the period on the **Per period** tab. You may click on the **Free selection** tab to select a specific date or a range of dates.

3) Click on the **OK** button.
