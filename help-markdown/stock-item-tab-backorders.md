# Stock items - Backorders tab

***

When processing invoices in **Documents** (**Default** ribbon), and the debtor (customer / client) orders more than is actually invoiced, it will be recorded in the backorder facility.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *Backorder records and reports will only be updated when the Invoices are posted (updated) to the ledger.* |
| --- | --- |


An example where the quantity entered in the “*Qty Order*” field is more than the quantity entered in the “*Qty Ship*” field, is as follows:

![Image](<lib/document-entry-backorder-quantities.png>)

| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | *You may also click on the **Backorder** icon to edit backorders when processing invoices and quotes.*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


The insufficient quantities (e.g. 10 – 2 = 8, as per this example) will be added to the backorder feature. The following options, on the **Default** ribbon, are available to manage (view / add / edit / delete) backorders:

1. **Stock items** (**Backorders** tab) – List of backorders for the selected stock item. &nbsp;
1. **Debtors** (**Backorders** tab) – List of backorders for the selected debtor (customer / client) account. &nbsp;
1. **Input → Backorders Outstanding** – List of backorders for all stock items.

The Backorder report may be printed in **Reports → Stock →&nbsp; Backorder** (**Reports** ribbon). This will launch the Backorders options screen on which you may select the range of Stock items to include / exclude from the report.

![Image](<lib/stock-tab-backorders.png>)

| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | ***Sorting and Rearranging Columns:*** *You can click on the column headings (such as Stock item, Description, Unit, Items, Discount, Stock, Referral source, Selling price, Price, and Exp. Selected) to change the sort sequence from ascending to descending order.* *To rearrange the sequence in which the columns are displayed, you can click and drag any of the column headings to the left or right.* |
| --- | --- |


**Add / Edit Backorders**

![Image](<lib/stock-tab-backorders-edit.png>)

When processing Invoices and you select the Stock item on backorder, the invoicing screen, the Backorders Outstanding screen is displayed:

![Image](<lib/document-entry-backorder-selection.png>)

If you select this item, the entry will be removed from the backorder records and reports.
