# IFRS for SME's - Countries - Optional / Mandatory

***

# In which countries is the IFRS for SME's optional and in which countries is it compulsory?

See - [IFRS for SMEs around the world](<https://www.ifrs.org/use-around-the-world/use-of-ifrs-standards-by-jurisdiction/> "target=\"\_blank\"") -

The International Financial Reporting Standards for Small and Medium-sized Entities (IFRS for SMEs) is not universally adopted and varies by country.

As in 2021, some countries have made the IFRS for SMEs mandatory for SMEs, while others have made it optional. Here are some examples:

**Optional**:

* Australia
* Canada
* India
* New Zealand
* South Africa

**Mandatory**:

* European Union member states (including the United Kingdom)
* China
* Hong Kong
* Malaysia
* Pakistan
* Singapore

Please note that this list may not be complete and that the adoption of IFRS for SMEs may change over time. It is recommended to consult with a professional accountant or regulatory body for up-to-date information on the adoption of IFRS for SMEs in a specific country.
