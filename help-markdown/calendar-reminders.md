# Reminders

***

In osFinancials5/TurboCASH5, the Calendar feature includes a Reminder functionality that helps you stay organised and on top of your scheduled events and appointments. Here are the key options available for managing reminders:![Image](<lib/calendar-appointment-reminders.png>)

1. **Dismiss**: By clicking on the Dismiss option, you can turn off the reminder for a specific event or appointment. If you no longer wish to receive reminders for that particular item, this option allows you to deactivate the reminder. If needed, you can always edit the event later to enable the reminder again.
1. **Snooze**: If you're not ready to address the reminder at that moment but would like to be reminded later, you can click on the **Snooze** option. This will postpone the reminder and provide you with options to set a customised time interval for the reminder to reappear. You can choose intervals such as 5, 10, 15, 30, or 45 minutes, as well as 1 to 8 hours, 1 to 6 days, or 1 week.
1. **Open item**: Clicking on the **Open item** option allows you to directly access and edit the event or appointment associated with the reminder. You can make any necessary changes, finalise or reschedule the appointment, and even activate or deactivate the reminder based on your preferences.

By utilising these reminder management options, you can efficiently handle your scheduled events and appointments, ensuring that you stay informed and can take appropriate actions when needed.
