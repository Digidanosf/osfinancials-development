### Important Localized Notes on Sales Taxes (GST/HST/PST)

> **Skelton Sales Tax Notice:** The structural accounts provided (G2310000, G2310900, G2320000) serve as your base skeleton. Because Canada uses varying tax frameworks across provinces and territories (HST, GST+PST, or GST alone), you must create specific regional sub-accounts to track your specific reporting liabilities. For instance:

> * **ON, NS, NB, NL, PE (HST):** Create sub-accounts for HST Collected and HST ITCs.
> * **BC, AB, SK, MB, QC (GST + PST/QST):** Create separate sub-accounts for GST Collected, GST ITCs, and a distinct account for PST/QST Payable (since PST/QST cannot be recovered as ITCs and must instead be capitalized or expensed on operational inputs).
