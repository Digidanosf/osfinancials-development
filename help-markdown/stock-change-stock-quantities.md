# Change stock quantites

***

Quantities on hand may be changed on the Stock edit form; or on the Stock item list screen. You will be prompted to enter a remark (reason / note) for changing the stock quantity on hand.

# Change Quantity on hand - Stock edit form

Select the stock item and click on the **Ledger** tab.

![Image](<lib/stock-change-quantities-03.png>)

If you change the "Quantity on hand" field on the Ledger tab, and you click on the **Save** button, a confirmation message is displayed:&nbsp;

*"Stock quantity has been changed\! Do you want to retain the current database data?"*&nbsp;

If you click on the **Yes** button, the Stock quantity on hand value will not be changed. It will revert to the old values.

To change the value with the new one entered in the "*Quantity on hand*" field, you need to click on the **No** button. The "*Change remark"* screen is displayed:

![Image](<lib/stock-change-remark.png>)

Enter a remark (comment) and click on the **OK** button.&nbsp;

# Change quantity on hand - Stock item grid

To be able to edit the values of the number of inventory items "**On hand**" column, you must select the [Advanced](<stock-item-grid-advanced-mode.md>) mode, so that you can edit the data as in a spreadsheet.

If you change the values of the stock quantity on hand in the quantity on hand column of the "*Stock item list*" screen, the confirmation message will not be displayed:

*"Stock quantity has been changed\! Do you want to retain the current database data?"*&nbsp;

![Image](<lib/stock-change-quantities-01.png>)

Once you have entered (changed) the quantity in the "*Quantity on hand*" column , the "*Change remark"* screen is displayed:

![Image](<lib/stock-change-remark.png>)

Enter a remark (comment) and click on the **OK** button.&nbsp;

# View - Change remarks on Stock item tabs

The remark as entered on the "*Change remark*" screen will be displayed in the [**Document groups**](<stock-item-tab-document-groups.md>) and [**Transactions**](<stock-item-tab-transactions.md>) tabs of the stock item.&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *The Document no. for a remark is automatically generated as a seven-digit number (consisting of a prefix **ST** followed by 5 numerical digits).* *The Document type is "Stock-"* |

## Open a Posted document&nbsp;

On the [**Document groups**](<stock-item-tab-document-groups.md>) and [**Transactions**](<stock-item-tab-transactions.md>) tabs, for stock items of which stock quantities is changed; you may right-click and select the "*Open document*" option on the context menu to open a selected posted document.

&nbsp;![Image](<lib/stock-change-quantities-open.png>)

The remark entered for the changed quantity on hand will be added in the "*Your reference*" field of the Document header.&nbsp;

## Print a Posted document&nbsp;

On the [**Document groups**](<stock-item-tab-document-groups.md>) tab, for stock items of which stock quantities is changed; you may may double-click to print a selected posted document.

![Image](<lib/stock-change-quantities-print.png>)
