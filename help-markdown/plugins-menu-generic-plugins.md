## Multimedia Pack

| ![Multi-media icon](<lib/B52.png>) | ***Multi Media Pack** - [Shop*](<https://www.osfinancials.org/en/webshop/most-sold/multimedia-pack> "target=\"\_blank\"") *- **Licence**: Once-off -* ***Documentation** : [Help topic*](<plugin-multimedia.md>)*.*&nbsp; ***Documentation : osFinancials website** - [Multimedia plugin*](<https://www.osfinancials.org/en/plugin-manuals/522-multimedia-plugin> "target=\"\_blank\"") *-*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


The Multimedia Plugin offers versatile functionality, allowing users to link various files within osFinancials5/TurboCASH5. Users can link files to debtors, creditors, stock items, and document groups. For instance, you can attach images to stock items or debtors, as well as link signed and scanned documents to debtors. Additionally,&nbsp;

if you have the Email Pro plugin, emails sent can also be linked to the corresponding documents and debtors, simplifying the process of locating emails related to specific clients. Furthermore, the Outlook Link Plugin can scan through your Outlook inbox and outbox to identify emails using the client's email address, automatically copying relevant emails to the multimedia section of the respective debtor or creditor for easy access.
