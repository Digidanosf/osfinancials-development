# Spreadsheet - General ledger - Budget - Column balances - This year

&nbsp;

***

You may import the budget totals, which have been added or edited on the [**Budget**](<budget-setup-accounts.md>) tab for selected accounts in **Setup → Accounts** (**Setup** ribbon) or on the [**Budgets**](<budgets-reports-ribbon.md>) icon (**Reports** ribbon), into a spreadsheet.

Reports for Budgets may also be printed on the **Reports&nbsp; → Ledger&nbsp; → [Budget**](<report-ledger-budget-actual.md>) menu; or on the [Budget listing](<budget-listing-reports.md>) reports on **Ledger analyser 1** or **Ledger analyser 2** selecting the "*Budget*" option.

**To import a budget for This year into a spreadsheet:**&nbsp;

1. On the **Reports** ribbon, select **Spreadsheet reports → General ledger → Budget → Column balances → This year**. The budget totals for each of this year periods will be listed in the "*Settings*" sheet of the spreadsheet:

![Image](<lib/spreadsheet-reports-budget-this-last-year-set.png>)

2. To view the budget figures for each account, click on the "*Column balances*" sheet of the spreadsheet:

![Image](<lib/spreadsheet-reports-budget-this-last-year.png>)

&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *This spreadsheet lists the totals for the main account as well as sub-accounts. In this example, note the following:* ***210000 - Motor vehicle - main account =&nbsp; 450.00**&nbsp; - The total of the following sub-accounts:* *210010 - Fuel and oil - sub account&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; = 100.00*&nbsp; *210020 - Maintenance - sub account&nbsp; &nbsp; = 150.00*&nbsp; *210030 - Repairs - sub account &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; = 200.00*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


The "*Column balances*" sheet of the spreadsheet will summarise the total of the budget figures for each general ledger account in the following columns:

* **Period 0** (Opening Last Year) - The totals of the opening budget totals for the previous year.
* **Periods 1 to 12** - The totals of budget figures entered in the respective periods for this year.
* **Total** - Total for each period (Period 1 -12) plus Period 0.
