# Export - Reports ribbon

## Ledger analyser - Financials

There are six (6) report types, which may be selected (i.e. [Trial balance](<ledger-analyser-trial-balance.md>), [Income statement](<ledger-analyser-income-statement.md>), [Balance sheet](<ledger-analyser-balance-sheet.md>), [Standard column balances](<ledger-analyser-standard-ccolumn.md>), [Budget vs actual](<ledger-analyser-budget-actual.md>) and [This year vs last year](<ledger-analyser-this-last-year.md>)) which may be printed and exported to spreadsheets.

## Column balances

In addition to this, there are 6 report types which may be exported to spreadsheets (i.e. [Column balances](<ledger-analyser-column-balances1.md>), [Column balances Debit/Credit](<ledger-analyser-column-balances2.md>), [Column balances debtors](<ledger-debtor-column-balances1.md>), [Column balances creditors](<ledger-creditor-column-balances1.md>), [Column balances debtors Debit/Credit](<ledger-debtor-column-balances2.md>), and [Column balances creditors Debit/Credit](<ledger-creditor-column-balances2.md>).)

## Budgets

Budget figures as entered or processed in the [**Budgets**](<budgets-reports-ribbon.md>) option on the [**Reports**](<budgets-reports-ribbon.md>) ribbon; or in **Setup → [Accounts**](<budget-setup-accounts.md>) (**Setup** ribbon). may be printed and exported.&nbsp; This option, if selected, will list only the budget figures (Budget listing) You may select the "*Budget*" option on the [Trial balance](<ledger-analyser-trial-balance.md>), [Income statement](<ledger-analyser-income-statement.md>), [Balance sheet](<ledger-analyser-balance-sheet.md>) and [Standard column balances](<ledger-analyser-standard-ccolumn.md>) report types. These budget figures may be printed or exported.&nbsp;

The [Budget vs actual](<ledger-analyser-budget-actual.md>) is the budget performance report. It will list and compare the budget figures versus the actual posted (updated) transactions. The [Budget vs actual](<ledger-analyser-budget-actual.md>) report may also be printed or exported.&nbsp;

## Transactions

[**T-Account viewer**](<t-account-viewer.md>) - You may select a specific account, group, etc. to view the transactions in the [**T-Account viewer**](<t-account-viewer.md>).&nbsp;

On the report types in the [Ledger analyser](<ledger-analyser.md>), you may right-click on a selected account, and select the “*Show details*” option on the context menu. You may print these transactions and exported these transactions to spreadsheets.

# Open item link

The lists on the Open item link - [Totals](<open-item-export-totals-tab.md>) tab, [Transactions](<open-item-export-transactions-ta.md>) tab and selected accounts may be exported and opened in spreadsheets.&nbsp;
