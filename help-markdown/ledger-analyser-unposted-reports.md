# Ledger analyser - Unposted batches

***

You may view, print and export a Trial balance, Income statement and Balance sheet for unposted as well as posted batches from the **Batch entry** (**F2**) context menu.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-warning.png>) | *Unposted Invoices, Credit notes, Purchases and Supplier returns is not included in this report.* |
| --- | --- |


| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | *You may go to the **Input →** [**Checking unposted items***](<checking-for-unposted-items.md>) *(**Default** ribbon) to view a list of unposted transactions in batches and documents.*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


**To print a Trial balance, Income statement or Balance sheet for unposted batches:**

1. On the **Default** ribbon, select **Batch entry** (**F2**).
1. Right-click and select "*Trial balance*" from the context menu.&nbsp;

![Image](<lib/batch-unposted-trial-balance-menu.png>)

3. A confirmation message is displayed:&nbsp;

*Unposted only?*

4. Click on the **Yes** button.

# | ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *If you click on the **No** button, the Trial balance, Income statement and Balance sheet for posted batches (excluding unposted documents) will be printed. These reports, as well as others, may be printed in the **Ledger analyser 1** or **Ledger analyser 2** on the **Reports** ribbon.* &nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


![Image](<lib/batch-unposted-trial-balance-report.png>)

# | ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *When this Trial balance is launched, the **Print** button will not be displayed. To initialise the **Print** button, select Income statement or Balance sheet and then Trial balance.* |
| --- | --- |


# | ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *If "Unposted only?" is selected, the **T-Account viewer** and the **Pivot** options will not have any data to display.* &nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


4. Click on the **Print** button. Only those unposted transactions (excluding unposted documents) will be printed.&nbsp;
