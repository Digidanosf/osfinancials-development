The Horeca POS plugin is a specialized checkout system designed specifically for catering establishments, such as restaurants, cafes, and bars. This plugin enhances the functionality of osFinancials by providing features tailored to the needs of the hospitality industry.

Here are some key features and functionalities of the Horeca POS plugin:

1. **Handheld Computer Support**: The plugin supports the use of handheld computers, which can speed up the process of serving customers and provide better control over the ordered products. Staff can take orders directly at the table using handheld devices.
1. **Ticket Printing**: After an order is recorded at a table, the plugin enables the printing of order tickets specific to the kitchen, bar, or the main point of sale (POS) computer. The printer specified for each product type will be used to print the order details.
1. **Product Options**: The plugin allows for the use of product options, such as specifying additional items like butter, mustard, or bread, to customize orders based on customer preferences or requirements.
1. **Quick Search Function**: Products can be easily selected through a quick search function based on the starting letter of the product name, making it efficient to locate and add items to the order.
1. **Visual Product Selection**: The plugin offers an icon-based interface that allows users to navigate through product groups and select products using visual representations. This can be particularly useful for quick and intuitive selection, especially in high-pressure environments.
1. **Floor Plan Management**: With the floor plan feature, the plugin enables the management of tables and seating arrangements. Users can indicate which tables are in use and how many place settings are occupied. Tables can be split or merged as needed, and the total order can be split into separate bills with the press of a button.
1. **Hold Orders**: After closing the active screen, orders can be placed on hold, allowing flexibility and the ability to manage multiple orders simultaneously.

The Horeca POS plugin is available for purchase with a support contract, and the multi-user plugin is provided for free. Upon payment, users will receive an unlocking code via email to activate the purchased plugin.

## HorecaPOS addition quantity on hand adjustment

![Image](<lib/horecapluginaanpassenaantallen.jpg>)- [*Manual*](<https://www.osfinancials.org/en/plugin-manuals/523-retail-pos-en> "target=\"\_blank\"") - [*Shop*](<https://www.osfinancials.org/en/webshop/register-system---pos/retail-pos-plugin> "target=\"\_blank\"") - Annual subscription - This plugin is available as part of an annual subscription plan, providing continuous access to the quantity on hand adjustment functionality.

This plugin provides functionality for employees to manually adjust the quantity on hand for specific items in the HorecaPOS system. This plugin is designed to streamline inventory management processes in the hospitality industry, allowing for accurate tracking of stock levels.

Here are the key features and details of the HorecaPOS addition quantity on hand adjustment plugin:

1. **Manual Quantity Adjustment**: The plugin enables employees to manually adjust the quantity on hand for specific items within the HorecaPOS system. This adjustment can be made based on actual stock counts or inventory movements.
1. **Shop-Level Functionality**: The plugin operates at the shop or location level, allowing adjustments to be made specifically for the inventory of that particular shop or location.
1. **Use Cases**: The plugin is particularly useful for situations where employees need to update the quantity on hand for specific items. For example, it can be used when items are taken out of the freezer, prepared for cooking, or made available for immediate use.

By utilising the HorecaPOS addition quantity on hand adjustment plugin, businesses in the hospitality industry can ensure accurate inventory management, avoid stockouts or overstocking, and maintain control over their stock levels. The plugin enhances the functionality of the HorecaPOS system by providing a streamlined process for manual adjustments to the quantity on hand for specific items.
