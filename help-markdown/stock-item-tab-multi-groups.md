# Stock items - Multi-group tab

***

This option allows you to set multiple groups to the selected stock item. The main Reporting group 1 (Stock group 1) is selected on the Reports Reporting group 1 section of the Edit Stock screen. The same list of the available Reporting group 1 (**Setup → [Groups**](<stock-item-reporting-groups.md>) (**Stock group 1**)) will be listed and may also be linked to the stock item.
