Please note that development or modification of osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 and document layouts are not included in the support contract.

# Conversion

If you need to convert your data from other accounting packages, the following options is available on the [osFinancials Webshop](<http://www.osfinancials.org/en/webshop/conversion> "target=\"\_blank\""):&nbsp;

## Conversion Basic&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/icon-osf-conversion-basic.png>) | ***Conversion Basic**&nbsp; - [Shop*](<https://www.osfinancials.org/en/webshop/conversion/conversion-basic-> "target=\"\_blank\"") *- Payment per hour -* |
| --- | --- |


With this product one of our technical consultants will convert the files of your present Set of Books to osFinancials with the maximum of time for 1 hour.

The files that will be converted are:

* &nbsp;
  * Debtor file
  * Creditor file
  * Stock file&nbsp;

Please note: This conversion doesn’t include the ledger accounts.

The submitted file must be in csv, xls, txt, or wk1 format.

If you want to submit an other format, please contact us.

## Conversion Extensive&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/icon-osf-conversion-extensive.png>) | ***Conversion Extensive**&nbsp; - [Shop*](<https://www.osfinancials.org/en/webshop/conversion/conversion-extensive> "target=\"\_blank\"") *- Payment per request -* |
| --- | --- |


With this product one of our technical consultants will convert the files of your present Set of Books to osFinancials5/TurboCASH5.

Converting data from various other accounting software packages to osFinancials5/TurboCASH5. Here's a breakdown of the process and what's involved:

1. **Conversion Service**: The conversion service offered by osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 involves one of their technical consultants converting files from your current accounting software to osFinancials5/TurboCASH5. This service has a maximum time limit of 2 hours.
1. **Supported Formats**: Data from your existing accounting software can be converted if it can be provided in CSV, XLS, TXT, or WK1 format. These are common file formats for exporting data from accounting software.
1. **Converted Files**: The following types of files can be converted as part of the service:
1) &nbsp;

   - **Debtor file**: Information about your customers or clients.
   - **Creditor file**: Information about your suppliers or vendors.
   - **Stock file**: Details about your inventory or stock items.
   - **Ledger accounts**: Your financial accounts, including income, expenses, assets, and liabilities.
   - **Mutations**: Changes or transactions recorded in your accounting system.

4. **Supported Software Packages**: The service supports conversion from several Dutch accounting software packages, including:
- &nbsp;

  - Accountview
  - ADBPlus
  - Davilex
  - Exact
  - Muis
  - Nedsof

5. **Contact for Inquiry**: If you're unsure whether your data can be converted or if you have any questions about the conversion process, you can contact osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 for clarification.

If you're considering migrating your accounting data to osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 and your current software is one of the supported packages, this conversion service could streamline the transition process for you. It's always a good idea to reach out to osFinancials directly for specific details about the conversion process and any requirements or considerations specific to your situation.
