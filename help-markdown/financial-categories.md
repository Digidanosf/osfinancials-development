# Options to link account groups to financial categories

Financial categories are a way to group accounts into categories that are relevant for financial reporting. In osFinancials5/TurboCASH5, financial categories are used to generate reports for the balance sheet and income statement. Financial categories include assets, liabilities, capital, income, and expenses.&nbsp;

When setting up accounts in osFinancials5/TurboCASH5, they can be linked to financial categories so that they can structure the financial reports. By properly categorizing accounts into financial categories, businesses can get a clear picture of their financial position and performance.

Once your Reporting groups for Account group 1 and/or Account group 2 is added, you need to link these to one of the Assets, Capital, Expenses, Income or Liabilities financial categories.

The following two (2) options, on the Setup ribbon, are available to link the financial categories:

1. **Setup → [Financial categories**](<financial-categories-setup.md>).
1. **Setup →&nbsp; [Accounts**](<financial-categories-accounts.md>).

The basic account groups should already be pre-set for a Set of Books, and you may add, edit or delete any of these groups in **Setup → [Groups**](<reporting-groups-setup-accounts.md>) on the Account group 1 and/or Account group 2, before linking them to financial categories.&nbsp;

* Assets, Capital and Liabilities are Balance sheet financial categories, and&nbsp;
* Income and Expenses are Income statement financial categories.&nbsp;
