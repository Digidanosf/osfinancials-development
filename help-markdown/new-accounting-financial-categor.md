# Account groups -  Financial categories

***

Each account included in the chart of accounts in osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 Sets of Books (templates) is linked to account groups, which in turn are linked to the five financial categories (INCOME, EXPENSES, CAPITAL, ASSETS, and LIABILITIES).

Each of these financial categories is further divided into groups that classify the financial transactions according to their nature. This provides the basic structure for the chart of accounts to generate various financial reports based on account number or account group 1 or account group 2.

The classification of financial categories and account groups for the income statement and balance sheet is briefly explained in the following topics:

* [Account groups - Financial categories - Income statement](<new-accounting-account-groups.md>) &nbsp;
* [Account groups - Financial categories - Balance sheet](<new-accounting-account-groups-ba.md>) &nbsp;
