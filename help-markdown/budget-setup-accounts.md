# Enter budget figures (Setup - Accounts)

***

**To enter / change your Budget figures:**

1. On the **Setup** ribbon, select **Setup → Accounts**.&nbsp;

2) Select the account to enter the budget figures.

![Image](<lib/budget-setup-acoounts.png>)

3. Select the first account, i.e. “*Sales*” and enter the budget figure. Enter the annual amount of **–**&#8202;*120 000* in the “*Total Amount"* field and click on the **Set period budget** button.&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *For a credit amount, insert minus sign “**–**“. The credit amount will be indicated with a minus sign after the amount (e.g. 10,000.00#8202;**–** in the "Amount" column.*&nbsp; *For a debit amount just insert the annual amount without the minus sign “**–**“. The amount will automatically be divided this amount into 12 months in the "Amount" field.*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *Each month can be overwritten so that the budget can be flexed month by month to accommodate seasonal trends and spending patterns of your business. The system will keep a running total for you, so that as you are flexing the monthly budget, you can see the effect on the annual amount.*&nbsp; &nbsp; *Budget figures can also be entered for for Balance sheet accounts (Capital, Liabilities and Assets), which may be used as a guideline to control the balances on these accounts.* *See - [Enter budget figures for special events*](<budget-setup-accounts-events.md>)*.* |
| --- | --- |


3. Click on the **Save** button (next to the account description field).&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *If budget figures already exist for the account, and you click on the **Save** button, a similar confirmation message will be displayed:*&nbsp; *"Delete old budget for year 2022?"* *Click **Yes** to replace the existing budget figures.*&nbsp; &nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *If you do not click on the **Save** button, any budget figures entered, or changed, will not be saved.*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


4. Select the next account and enter the budget figure.&nbsp;
4. Continue to enter your budget figures for all accounts.&nbsp;
4. When you are finished entering all your budget figures, click on the **Close** button (on the titlebar).
