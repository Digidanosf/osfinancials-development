# Open item link - Export to spreadsheets

***

The Open item link, matching your filter and search criteria, may be exported and opened in spreadsheets: 

* The [Totals](<open-item-link-totals-tab.md>) tab will list the transaction total and total outstanding total for each open item account.
* The [Transactions](<open-item-link-transactions-tab.md>) tab will list all transactions for each open item account. It will also list the outstanding amount (due amount) for each transaction. 
* [Selected accounts](<open-item-link-selected-accounts.md>) - The  **Export** icon will export the transaction totals and outstanding amounts for a specific [ledger account](<open-item-link-ledger-accounts.md>), [debtor (customer / client) account](<open-item-link-debtor-accounts.md>) or a [creditor (supplier / vendor) account](<open-item-link-creditor-accounts.md>).
