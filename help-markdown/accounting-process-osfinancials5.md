# Accounting process in osFinancials5/TurbCASH5-4

***

If you choose to work with one of the pre-created Sets of Books in osFinancials5/TurboCASH5, most of the settings and defaults have already been set.&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | *You may download an existing template for your country and language (in a matter of a few seconds about 3 clicks) from the [Let osFinancials help you to create a Set of Books - Download*](<create-books-default-download.md>) *option /&nbsp; [Let TurboCASH help you to create a Set of Books - Download*](<create-books-default-download.md>) *option.* &nbsp; *Each of these Sets of Books is already set to a specific language for your country and language.*&nbsp; *See a list of available Firebird [Sets of Books and linked languages available as a free download*](<sets-of-books-download-templates.md>)*. You may then Customise the Set of Books to meet your own requirements.*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


Getting started entails little more than the entry of a few basic details and the selection of some basic options:

1. [**Create your Set of Books**](<create-set-of-books.md>) - osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 allows you to create a Set of Books within minutes by copying an existing structure.
1. **Settings and defaults** - osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 will use these parameters and defaults to display and perform certain functions for you. &nbsp;

   1. [**Company info**](<setup-company-info.md>) **-** Enter your contact information and registration details and set the defaults for your Tax (if you are registered for Tax (VAT/GST/Sales tax)).
   1. [**Reporting dates**](<setup-reporting-dates.md>) **-** Set the starting date for your financial year and the accounting periods for which you may generate financial reports. &nbsp; &nbsp;
   1. [**Reporting groups**](<setup-groups.md>) **-** Add or edit any reporting account groups for your Accounts to further classify the five Financial categories. You may also add up to two (2) reporting groups for your Debtor (customer / client) accounts, Creditor (supplier / vendor) accounts, Stock items (products), Documents, Salespersons.
   1. [**Financial categories**](<financial-categories.md>) **-** Link your Reporting account groups (e.g. Income, Other income, Expenses, Capital, Long-term liabilities, Current liabilities, Fixed assets and Current assets) to the 5 Financial categories (i.e. Income, Expenses, Capital, Assets and Liabilities). &nbsp;
   1. [**Batch types**](<batch-types-setup.md>) **-** Add, edit or delete any batch types (journals) in which you wish to enter transactions.
   1. [**Access control**](<access-control.md>) and **passwords (Optional) -** Set passwords and access control for your users.
   1. [**Internet**](<setup-internet.md>) (Optional) **-** Configure your e-mail account to send reports, lists and documents over the Internet.
   1. [**Documents setup**](<documents-setup.md>) **-** Set the Document numbers, messages and check the Batch types for your sales documents (Invoices, Credit notes and Quotes for debtors (customers / clients) and purchase documents (Purchases, Supplier returns and Orders for creditors (suppliers / vendors).&nbsp;

You may also add a standard message (on the [**Statement setup**](<documents-setup-statements-setup.md>) tab) to print on all [**statements**](<debtor-statements.md>) you wish to send to your debtors (customers / clients).

1. &nbsp;
   9. [**Salespersons**](<setup-salespersons.md>) **-** Add any salespersons that may sell your stock or products.
   9. [**Stock information**](<stock-information.md>) **-** Define your selling prices and units of stock. You also need to set the [Cost of sales](<stock-create-cost-of-sales.md>) settings for your stock items / inventory items / products.
   9. **Set preferences -**

      1. [**Printer**](<setup-printer.md>) - Set the default printer and paper sizes.
      1. [**System** **parameters**](<setup-system-parameters.md>) - Set the defaults for both entry and display.

1. [**Customise your Chart of Accounts**](<ledger-accounts.md>) - Your Set of Books will have a standard Chart of Accounts, which should reflect all your accounts.
1. [**Configure your Tax (VAT/GST/Sales tax) to according to your Tax Registration**](<tax-registration-options.md>) - The status of your Tax (VAT/GST/Sales tax) registration with your Tax authorities, jurisdiction may need some adjustments in the Chart of Accounts.
1. Add [**Debtor**](<debtor-accounts.md>) (customer / client) accounts.
1. Add [**Creditor**](<creditor-accounts.md>) (supplier / vendor) accounts.
1. Add [**Stock items**](<stock-inventory.md>) (Inventory items / products) - Add your stock items / inventory items / products.
1. Enter Opening Balances for your accounts and trading stock items, if you have any.
1. Start processing your daily transactions in [batches (journals)](<batches-journals.md>) and or [documents](<documents.md>).
1. Print [reports](<reports-ribbon-options.md>) to evaluate your activities.
