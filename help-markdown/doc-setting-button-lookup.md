# Button lookup

***

By default, the Stock lookup are the default. To change this to be replaced by the Button lookup, select (tick) this option. This should support Touchscreen technology.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | ***Groups - Images and Colours*** *The Button lookup may be configured by adding images of your product groups and setting colours (background and font) in **Setup → [Groups***](<stock-item-reporting-groups.md>) *(**Stock group 1**).*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | ***Product - Images*** *The Button lookup may be configured by adding images of your products on the Images tab of Stock items in the **Stock items** (**Default** ribbon).*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


The button lookup will display images added in the following options:

* **Setup → [Groups**](<stock-item-reporting-groups.md>) (**Stock group 1** tab) (**Setup** ribbon). These images will display as Stock groups on the Button lookup.&nbsp;
* **Stock items** (**Images** tab) (**Default** ribbon). These images will display as Product images on the Button lookup

These images may be used on a touch screen.&nbsp;

**To enable (activate) the Button lookup:**

1. Go to the **Setup → Stock information**.&nbsp;
1. Select (tick) the “Button lookup” field.&nbsp;

***

3. Click on the **OK** button. The Button lookup screen is displayed when selecting stock items while processing documents.&nbsp;
