# [osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 Business Class](<https://www.osfinancials.org/en/latest-news/636-osfinancials-5-business-en> "target=\"\_blank\"")

After years of development and support of osFinancials and TurboCASH we came to the conclusion the current model does not work. To break through this glass ceiling we are introducing osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 Business Class.

osFinancials 4 / TurboCASH 4 is free to use but if you want more out of your software then osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 Business Class is the version you want to use.

If you have less then 500 transactions / documents you can use this version free of charge. Above that we ask you for a contribution of 50 euro (excl.)/ 60 dollars per year.

If you have any subscription already like multi-user or retail POS, etc., the licence will also be included.

# Included in the registered version of osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 - Business Class

## osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 Subscription

Process unlimited (more than 500) transactions and documents.&nbsp;

Every time an unregistered version osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 is started, the following confirmation message will be displayed:

*This software is limited to 500 transactions or 500 documents. Please register and buy a licence for osFinancials*\
*This copy of osFinancials is not registered\! Do you wish to register it now?*

Unregistered versions will allow you to process up to 500 transactions and /or documents. Once this limit is reached, osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 will not allow any processing of transactions, in batches and/or documents.&nbsp;

Once you have registered osFinancials5/TurboCASH5, you will receive an unlocking code and will be able to process further transactions and or documents. &nbsp;

## Bank import&nbsp;

| ![Bank import plugin icon](<lib/icon-osf-shop-bank.png>) | ***BankIimport Plugin** -*&nbsp; ***Licence** : Included in osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 Subscription.*&nbsp; *Documentation : [Help topic*](<plugin-bank-import.md>)*.* *Documentation : osFinancials website - [Bank import plugin*](<https://www.osfinancials.org/en/plugin-manuals/428-bank-import-plugin> "target=\"\_blank\"") *-*&nbsp; *Documentation : osFinancials website - [Bank Reconciliations Plugin*](<https://www.osfinancials.org/en/plugin-manuals/512-bank-reconciliations-plugin> "target=\"\_blank\"") *-*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


The Bank Import plugin included with the osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 Subscription is a handy tool for managing and processing the transactions in your bank and credit card accounts. You can download electronic bank statement files directly from your online banking services at your bank or financial institution. After that, you can use the Bank Import plugin to import these bank statements into your bank accounts. This process saves you a lot of time and effort as you do not have to manually enter and process the transactions of your bank accounts and credit card accounts in separate Payment journals and Receipt journals.&nbsp;

Additionally, the Bank Import Plugin has the ability to use bank references to automatically assign transactions to the appropriate accounts, This automation not only saves time, but also reduces the risk of human error associated with manual data entry. Overall, using this Bank Import plugin is a smart choice for businesses or individuals who want to optimize their financial workflow within osFinancials5/TurboCASH5.

## Budget Plugin

| ![Image](<lib/budgetten.jpg>) | ***Budget Plugin** - [Manual*](<https://www.osfinancials.org/en/plugin-manuals/529-budget-plugin> "target=\"\_blank\"") *- [Shop*](<https://www.osfinancials.org/en/webshop/branch-specific-solutions/budget-plugin> "target=\"\_blank\"") *- [Help documentation*](<budgets.md>)&nbsp; ***Licence** : Included in osFinancials5 Subscription / TurboCASH5 Subscription.*&nbsp; |

Effortlessly manage budgets for accounts, cost centres, and projects with the Budget Plugin, now included as a standard feature in the osFinancials5 Subscription / TurboCASH5 Subscription. Access the [**Budgets**](<budgets-reports-ribbon.md>) option on the **Reports** ribbon for seamless integration.

**Key Features**:

* **Flexible Budget Entry:** Easily input budgets for accounts, cost centres, and projects directly within the osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 interface. Copy budget figures from the previous year of actual account balances.
* **Comprehensive Budgeting:** Cover all aspects of financial planning by setting budgets for various entities, including accounts, cost centres, and projects.
* **Integrated Solution:** Seamlessly integrate budget management into your overall financial workflow within osFinancials5/TurboCASH5.
* **Advanced Filtering and Pivot Options:** Utilize advanced filtering and pivot functionalities to analyse budget data effectively and gain deeper insights into your financial performance.
* **Streamlined Processes:** Simplify budgeting tasks and ensure accuracy by leveraging the intuitive interface and advanced features of the Budget Plugin.
* **Comprehensive Reporting:** Generate budget listing reports (Trial balance, Income statement, Balance sheet, or Standard column balances report types) and budget performance reports (Budget vs Actual to compare the budget figures versus the actual posted transactions). Export reports to spreadsheets and utilize powerful pivot features for in-depth analysis. Print and export budgets in the T-Account viewer.

Enhance your financial planning capabilities and streamline your budgeting processes with the Budget Plugin, empowering you to make informed decisions and drive financial success within your organisation.
