# Company info (reports menu)

***

The following two (2) reports are available to print information for the Set of Books:

* **Company info – Bank accounts** - Lists the details of the company bank accounts details as entered in **Setup → Company info**.&nbsp;
* **Company info - Information** - Lists the document headings and messages for each document type as well as statement message as specified or entered in **Setup → Documents setup**. It also lists the Selling price descriptions and Cost of sales settings as entered and set on the **Setup → Stock information**.&nbsp;

# Company info report options

**To print Company info reports:**&nbsp;

1. On the **Reports** ribbon, select **Reports → Company** info.&nbsp;

&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp; ![Image](<lib/reports-company-info-options.png>) &nbsp;

2. "*Bank accounts*" is the default report. You may select the "*Information*" report.
2. Click **OK**. &nbsp;

# Printed : Company info - Information&nbsp;

An example of the "*Information*" report, is as follows:

&nbsp;![Image](<lib/reports-company-info-message-setings-print.png>)

Lists the details of the following:

* **Information** - **Document heading and messages** (up to 3 lines) as entered in the **Documents setup** (**Setup** ribbon) for each of the Sales documents (i.e. **Invoices**, **Credit notes** and **Quotes** tabs) as well as Purchase documents (i.e. **Purchases**, **Supplier returns** and **Orders** tabs). This information is standard and will be printed on all documents.&nbsp;
* **Global statement** message (up to 3 lines) as entered on the **Statement setup** tab on **Documents setup** (**Setup** ribbon). This information will print standard on all **Statements** **(Reports → Debtors – Outstanding** (**Reports** ribbon)). This is in addition to the specific messages (up to 4 lines) that can be entered in the **Delivery address** tab individual debtor accounts (**Debtors** - **Default** ribbon).
* **Selling price descriptions** – The descriptions of Selling price 1, Selling price 2 and Selling price 3 as entered in the Selling price descriptions fields (**Stock information** - **Setup** ribbon).
* **Cost of Sales** –&nbsp;

  * **Create Cost of Sales** - **Yes** - Cost of Sales is activated.

&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; - **No** - Cost of Sales is not activated.&nbsp;

* &nbsp;
  * **Average cost** - **Yes** - Average Cost of Sales is activated.

&nbsp;&nbsp; - **No** - Latest Cost of Sales is activated.&nbsp;
