# Localisation-Batches-Serials

| ![Image](<lib/B84.png>) | ***Localisation-Batches-Serials** - [Manual*](<https://www.osfinancials.org/en/plugin-manuals/540-locations-batches-and-serials-plugin-en> "target=\"\_blank\"") *- [Shop*](<https://www.osfinancials.org/en/webshop/stock/serial-batch-location> "target=\"\_blank\"") *- **Licence**: Once-off*&nbsp; ***Documentation** : [Help documentation*](<plugin-serials-batches-location.md>)&nbsp; ***Translations** : [Outstanding*](<translate-plugins-batch-serial-l.md>)&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


Efficiently manage your product inventory with our Localisation-Batches-Serials Plugin. With a one-time licence, this plugin enables you to track the location, batches, or serial numbers of your stock items when processing documents such as purchases and invoices.

**Key Features**:

* &nbsp;
  * Track product location, batches, or serial numbers
  * Enhance inventory management during document processing
  * Ideal for maintaining accurate records of stock items

**Reports:** Access several reports under **Reports → Plugins → Locations-Batches-Serials**, including:

* &nbsp;
  * **On hand**: Lists serials or batches available for sale to customers.
  * **Stock movement**: Lists serials or batches purchased from suppliers and sold to customers.
  * **Bought from supplier**: Lists serials or batches purchased from suppliers.
  * **Sold to customer**: Lists serials or batches sold to customers.
  * **Outdated batch**: Lists serials or batches that are still on hand but past the maximum sales date.

This plugin provides comprehensive inventory tracking and management capabilities within the osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 software, ensuring efficient control over your stock items.

# Quick Order

| ![Image](<lib/B45.png>) | ***Quick Order** - Manual - [Shop*](<https://www.osfinancials.org/en/webshop/stock/stock-quick-order-plugin> "target=\"\_blank\"") *-&nbsp; **Licence**: Once-off*&nbsp; ***Translations** : [Translations outstanding*](<translate-plugins-stock-check1.md>)&nbsp; ***Documentation** : [Help documentation*](<plugin-stock-quick-order.md>)&nbsp; ***Translations** : [Outstanding*](<translate-plugins-stock-check1.md>)&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


Simplify your product ordering process with our "*Quick Order*" plugin. This plugin offers a quick and convenient method for ordering products from your PC or using a handheld PC or smart phone (through Remote Desktop / Terminal Services or RDP).

* &nbsp;
  * You walk with a handheld through your warehouse, and scan or selected the product to order while standing next to the shelf.
  * You see immediately actual stock numbers so that a good decision of the quantity to order can be made.
  * After pressing a button it creates an order with the specified numbers.
  * After which only a few clicks of the mouse will break down the order by vendor.

Optimize your ordering workflow with the "*Quick Order*" plugin, providing you with a seamless and efficient solution for managing your product orders.

# Stock check / Stock correction plugin

| ![Image](<lib/B38.png>) | ***Stock check / Stock correction** - Manual - [Shop*](<https://www.osfinancials.org/en/webshop/stock/stock-correction-plugin> "target=\"\_blank\"") *- **Licence**: Once-off*&nbsp; ***Translations** : [Translations outstanding*](<translate-plugins-stock-check.md>)&nbsp; ***Documentation** : [Help documentation*](<plugin-stock-check.md>) &nbsp; ***Translations** : [Outstanding*](<translate-plugins-stock-check.md>) &nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


This "*Stock correction*" plugin allows users to perform inventory counts conveniently from various devices such as PCs, handheld PCs, or smartphones. This can be done either through Remote Desktop (RDP) or Terminal Services.

Upon pressing a **Generate document** button, the plugin automatically generates a credit note with the incorrect numbers. This feature helps in documenting discrepancies in stock counts. After the credit note is generated, the plugin proceeds to correct the stock numbers based on the inventory count performed. This ensures that the inventory records are accurate and up to date.

**New Android Stock Count**.

The "*New Android Stock Count*"&nbsp; at an extra cost of 200,00 euros, excluding tax, allows users to connect to the plugin via their local network using an Android device.&nbsp; The Android version of the plugin supports barcode scanning functionality. Users can use a Bluetooth-enabled barcode scanner to scan barcodes of the stock items, which facilitates faster and more accurate stock counting.
