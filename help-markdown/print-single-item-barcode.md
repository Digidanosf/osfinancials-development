# Barcode - Stock grid reports

The "*Barcode*" report prints barcodes for all stock items or a selected stock item.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | *You may select the Barcode report on the **Reports → Stock → [Print labels***](<report-print-stock-barcodes.md>) *menu (**Reports** ribbon) to print barcodes.&nbsp; You may select a specific stock item or a range of stock items; or all stock items linked to a specific Stock reporting group 1.*&nbsp; &nbsp; |
| --- | --- |
