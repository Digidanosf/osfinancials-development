# Forum

***

You may access and view the latest and older [Forum](<https://www.osfinancials.org/en/forum-en> "target=\"\_blank\"") posts as well as various categories. Should you have a query or an issue, you may search and will probably find a solution to your query or issue.&nbsp;

If not, you may post your query or issue. Registered osFinancials users will soon help by posting answers or solutions to your query or issue.

**To access the forum:**

On the **Start** ribbon, select **Help → Forum**.&nbsp;

An information message will be displayed for unregistered users.

*"You are not yet registered to post on our forum\! You will need to register osFinancials to use that username password to login to our forum or shop."* &nbsp;

The [Forum](<https://www.osfinancials.org/en/forum-en> "target=\"\_blank\"") webpage is launched.&nbsp;
