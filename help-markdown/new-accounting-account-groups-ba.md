# Account groups - Financial categories - Balance sheet

***

Each of the Balance sheet Financial categories CAPITAL, ASSETS and LIABILITIES, are further classified or grouped as follows:

| ***FINANCIAL CATEGORY*** | ***REPORTING GROUPS FOR ACCOUNTS*** | ***DESCRIPTION*** | ***DR / CR*** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| ![Image](<lib/admon-material-accounts-capital.svg>)*CAPITAL* | *CAPITAL*&nbsp; *MEMBERS' CONTRIBUTION* *SHARE CAPITAL* | *The value of the owners’ equity.*&nbsp; *Sole Proprietor or Partnership* *Close Corporation* *Companies* | ***CREDIT*** |
| ![Image](<lib/admon-material-accounts-assets.svg>)*ASSETS* | *FIXED ASSETS*&nbsp; &nbsp; *CURRENT ASSETS* | *Physical items with a value,* *e.g. Land and Buildings, Plant and Machinery, Motor Vehicles, Furniture and Fittings, Computers, etc.*&nbsp; *Cash in the Bank, Petty cash, and Value of the Debtors ledger, stock holding and prepaid expenses.* | ***DEBIT*** |
| ![Image](<lib/admon-material-accounts-liabilities.svg>)*LIABILITIES* | *LONG-TERM LIABILITIES* &nbsp; *CURRENT LIABILITIES* | *All amounts owed to third parties or financial obligations repayable normally more than one year.*&nbsp; *All amounts owed by the business to Creditors, Taxes payable, accounts payable and provisions for expenses.* | ***CREDIT*** |


Each Balance sheet account type created in osFinancials should be assigned to an appropriate REPORTING ACCOUNT GROUP and linked to a FINANCIAL CATEGORY in osFinancials5/TurboCASH5.
