# Graph - Orders per month

***

| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | *The [Graph*](<documents-graphs.md>) *feature for each [document type*](<document-types.md>) *(i.e Orders) in **Documents** on the **Default** ribbon, allows you to filter, analyse and view your Orders in a graph using various options to include or exclude in the graph, i.e.:*&nbsp; [*Document types*](<document-types.md>) *- Select the Orders document type.*&nbsp; [*Document status*](<document-status.md>) *- Select All, Posted, Unposted (Open), Confirmed Orders.*&nbsp; [*Filter options*](<documents-filter-search-options.md>) *- Select the various options to include or exclude data to be included in the graph, etc.*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


The following two (2) graph reports to analyse Orders, which are **posted** and/or **confirmed**, for a specific period, may be printed:

* **Graph - Orders per month - Quantities** - displays the total quantities of items on the Quantity Shipped field of **Orders** per month in a bar chart.&nbsp;
* **Graph - Orders per month - Amount** - displays the total cost price amount (exclusive of tax (VAT/GST/Sales tax)) of **Orders** per month in a bar chart.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *This graph only includes Orders which are posted and/or confirmed. Unconfirmed or open Orders will be **NOT** be included in this graph.* |
| --- | --- |


# Graph - Orders per month options

**To print a Graph - Orders per month:**&nbsp;

1. On the **Reports** ribbon, select **User reports → Graphs → Graphs per month → Purchases → Orders per period**.&nbsp;

![Image](<lib/graphs-purchase-orders-per-month-options.png>)

2. Select the following:
1) &nbsp;

   1) **Report** - Select the **Graph - Orders per month - Quantities** or **Graph - Orders per month - Amount** report.
   1) **Period** - Select a period on the **Per period** tab. You may click on the **Free selection** tab to select a specific date or a range of dates to include in the graph.&nbsp;

3. Click on the **OK** button.&nbsp;

# Printed : Graph - Orders per month - Quantities

An example of the "*Graph - Orders per month - Quantities*", is as follows:

![Image](<lib/graphs-purchase-orders-per-month-print-1.png>)

The details is as follows:

1. **Vertical Axis** - The total quantities on Orders per month.&nbsp; &nbsp;
1. **Horizontal Axis** - This is the Year and Month ("*YYYYMM*" format) in the calendar year. &nbsp;
1. **Totals -** **Quantities** - The total quantities of all Orders for the selected period.

# Printed : Graph - Orders per month - Amount

An example of the "*Graph - Orders per month - Amount*", is as follows:

![Image](<lib/graphs-purchase-orders-per-month-print-2.png>)

The details is as follows:

1. **Vertical Axis** - The total amount for Orders per month.&nbsp; &nbsp;
1. **Horizontal Axis** - This is the Year and Month ("*YYYYMM*" format) in the calendar year. &nbsp;
1. **Exclusive total** - The total amount (exclusive of tax (VAT/GST/Sales tax)) of all Orders for the selected period.
