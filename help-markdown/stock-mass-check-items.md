# Mass check

***

This option will run a check on all stock items.&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *This process may take some time.* |
| --- | --- |


**To do a mass check on stock items:**&nbsp;

1. On the **Default** ribbon, select **Stock** **items**.
1. Select "*Mass check*" option on the context menu.&nbsp;
1. The stock item form will be automatically opened and closed for each stock item.&nbsp;
1. When the mass check process is finished, a confirmation message is displayed:

*"Check finished:"*

5. Click **OK**.&nbsp;
