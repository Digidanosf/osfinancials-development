# Financial categories (Setup - Accounts)

***

In osFinancials5/TurboCASH5, you can create up to two (2) reporting groups for your accounts. Account groups allow you to categorize and group your accounts based on how you want to report on them. Each account group needs to be linked to a financial category in order to structure the financial reports such as the trial balance, income statement, and balance sheet. By properly categorizing and grouping your accounts, you can more easily analyse and understand your financial data.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | *Account reporting groups may also be linked to financial categories on the **Setup → Financial categories** (**Setup** ribbon).* |
| --- | --- |


**To link Groups to Financial categories using the Accounts screen:**

1. On the **Setup** ribbon, select **Setup → Accounts**. The Groups as created on Account groups - Reporting group 1 will be listed in the "*Accounts*" screen.
1. Select "*Financial categories*" in the "*Sort*" field.

![Image](<lib/setup-accounts-financial-categories.png>)

3. Select a Group and select a Financial category (i.e. Assets, Capital, Expenses, Income or Liabilities) from the list.
3. Click on the **Save** button.
3. Repeat the process for all the Account groups.
3. Once finished with Reporting group 1, select Reporting group 2 and repeat the link process.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *The **Save** button will only be displayed (next to the Financial category name) field, once any&nbsp; any changes are made.* *You need to click on the **Save** button. If you do not do this, your changes will be lost.* |
