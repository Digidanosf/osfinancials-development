# User reports (Reports ribbon)

***

The reports on the **User reports** menu is included in osFinancials5/TurboCASH5.&nbsp;

Users may add their own reports in the *...\\plug\_ins\\reports\\userreports* folder.

![Image](<lib/user-reports-menu.png>)

The following user reports is included in osFinancials5/TurboCASH5:

1. [**Custom reports**](<user-reports-custom-reports.md>) – This is custom sales and purchase reports.

   1. &#50;cred-sku-det - Lists the Purchase and Supplier return documents for a selected creditor (supplier / vendor).&nbsp;
   1. &#50;deb-sku-det - Lists the Invoice and Credit note documents for a selected debtor (customer / client).

1. [**Tax**](<user-reports-tax-list-rates.md>) - List the Tax type account codes and the tax rates which is added / edited in **Setup → [Accounts**](<tax-accounts-add-edit.md>) (**Setup** ribbon). This report is useful to check your tax percentages after import of ledger accounts or if you customise your in Set of Books.
1. **Debtors** - [**Outstanding**](<reports-debtors-outstanding.md>) -&nbsp; “[*Due date*](<debtors-outstanding-due-date.md>)” and “[*Debtors outstanding at date*](<debtors-outstanding-at-date.md>)” reports. These reports will list all the transactions before and on a selected period (date). It will list the details for each transaction (i.e. Reference No., Date, Due date, Debit, Credit and the accumulative balance for each of the debtor (customer / client) account.
1. **Creditors** - [**Outstanding**](<reports-creditors-outstanding.md>) - “[*Due date*](<creditors-outstanding-due-date.md>)” and “[*Creditors outstanding at date*](<creditors-outstanding-at-date.md>)” reports. These reports will list all the transactions before and on a selected period (date). It will list the details for each transaction (i.e. Reference No., Date, Due date, Debit, Credit and the accumulative balance for each of the creditor (supplier) account.
