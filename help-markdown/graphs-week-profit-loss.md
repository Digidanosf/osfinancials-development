# Graph - Profit / Loss per week

***

The "*Graph - Profit / Loss per week*" displays the Net Profit and Loss per week in a bar chart.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *This graph only includes batch and document transactions which are posted. Unposted batches and documents will be **NOT** be included in this graph.* *To view a list of unposted batches and documents, which is not updated to the ledger, go to **Input → [Checking unposted items***](<checking-for-unposted-items.md>) *(**Default** ribbon).* |
| --- | --- |


# Graph - Profit / Loss per week options

**To print a Ledger - Graph report:**&nbsp;

1. On the **Reports** ribbon, select **User reports → Graphs → Graphs per week → Ledger**.&nbsp;

![Image](<lib/graphs-ledger-per-week-options.png>)

2. Select the **Period** - Select a period on the **Per period** tab. You may click on the **Free selection** tab to select a specific date or a range of dates to include in the graph.&nbsp;
2. Click on the **OK** button.&nbsp;

# Printed : Graph - Profit / Loss per week

An example of the "*Graph - Profit / Loss per week*", is as follows:

![Image](<lib/graphs-ledger-per-week-print.png>)

| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *The net profit / loss should balance with the net profit / loss on the trial balance and income statement, if the same periods are selected.* |
| --- | --- |


The details are as follows:

1. **Vertical Axis** - Positive amounts are Income (sales, etc.) and negative amounts are expenses, etc.&nbsp;
1. **Horizontal Axis** - This is the Number of the Week in the calendar year (1 to 52).&nbsp;
1. **Income / Expenses / Net Profit Loss** - The figures for the selected period. Net Profit is a positive figure and Net Loss is a negative figure.&nbsp;
