# osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 Plugins and Products

osFinancials5/TurboCASH5, is a comprehensive accounting software designed to meet your specific business needs. The osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 Subscription already includes three plugins. (ie; [Bank Import](<plugin-bank-import.md>), [Document Email Pro](<plugin-document-mailpro.md>) and [Budgets](<budgets-reports-ribbon.md>)).

Enhance your experience with our suite of products and plug-ins, which can be seamlessly integrated into osFinancials to extend its functionality beyond core features.

Our plugins are categorized into seven main groups: [Document plugins](<plugins-menu-documents.md>), [External system links](<plugins-menu-external-links.md>), [Financial tools](<plugins-menu-financial-tools.md>), [Generic plugins](<plugins-menu-generic-plugins.md>), [Point-of-Sale](<plugins-menu-pos.md>), [Stock plugins](<plugins-menu--stock.md>), and [Tools](<plugins-menu-tools.md>).

Whether you need enhanced reporting, task automation, industry-specific solutions or integration with other software, our plug-in programs offer flexibility and customization options to tailor osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 to your unique requirements.

Visit our [webshop](<https://www.osfinancials.org/en/webshop> "target=\"\_blank\"") to explore our products, including multi-user options, support, other products and more, and to optimize your osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 experience.

# Included in the registered version of osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 - Business Class

## osFinancials5 Subscription /TurboCASH Subscription

Process unlimited (more than 500) transactions and documents.&nbsp;

Every time an unregistered version osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 is started, the following confirmation message will be displayed:

*This software is limited to 500 transactions or 500 documents. Please register and buy a licence for osFinancials*\
*This copy of osFinancials is not registered\! Do you wish to register it now?*

Unregistered versions will allow you to process up to 500 transactions and /or documents. Once this limit is reached, osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 will not allow any processing of transactions, in batches and/or documents.&nbsp;

Once you have registered osFinancials5/TurboCASH5, you will receive an unlocking code and will be able to process further transactions and or documents. &nbsp;

The Bank Import plugin included with the osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 Subscription is a handy tool for managing and processing the transactions in your bank and credit card accounts. You can download electronic bank statement files directly from your online banking services at your bank or financial institution. After that, you can use the Bank Import plugin to import these bank statements into your bank accounts. This process saves you a lot of time and effort as you do not have to manually enter and process the transactions of your bank accounts and credit card accounts in separate Payment journals and Receipt Jjurnals.&nbsp;

Additionally, the Bank Import Plugin has the ability to use bank references to automatically assign transactions to the appropriate accounts, This automation not only saves time, but also reduces the risk of human error associated with manual data entry. Overall, using this Bank Import plugin is a smart choice for businesses or individuals who want to optimize their financial workflow within osFinancials5/TurboCASH5.

Effortlessly manage budgets for accounts, cost centres, and projects with the Budget Plugin, now included as a standard feature in the osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 Subscription. Access the [**Budgets**](<budgets-reports-ribbon.md>) option on the **Reports** ribbon for seamless integration.

**Key Features**:

* **Flexible Budget Entry:** Easily input budgets for accounts, cost centres, and projects directly within the osFinancials5 interface. Copy budget figures from the previous year of actual account balances.
* **Comprehensive Budgeting:** Cover all aspects of financial planning by setting budgets for various entities, including accounts, cost centres, and projects.
* **Integrated Solution:** Seamlessly integrate budget management into your overall financial workflow within osFinancials5/TurboCASH5.
* **Advanced Filtering and Pivot Options:** Utilize advanced filtering and pivot functionalities to analyse budget data effectively and gain deeper insights into your financial performance.
* **Streamlined Processes:** Simplify budgeting tasks and ensure accuracy by leveraging the intuitive interface and advanced features of the Budget Plugin.
* **Comprehensive Reporting:** Generate budget listing reports (Trial balance, Income statement, Balance sheet, or Standard column balances report types) and budget performance reports (Budget vs Actual to compare the budget figures versus the actual posted transactions). Export reports to spreadsheets and utilize powerful pivot features for in-depth analysis. Print and export budgets in the T-Account viewer.

Enhance your financial planning capabilities and streamline your budgeting processes with the Budget Plugin, empowering you to make informed decisions and drive financial success within your organisation.

# Plugin activation and Plugins menu

1. [Activate plugins](<plugins-activate.md>) - Allows you to manage plugins listed in the **Plugins** menu on the **Setup** ribbon. Clicking on the **Activate plugins** button in the **Setup** ribbon allows you to add plugins to the **Plugins** menu. To remove a plugin from the menu, click on the **Deactivate plugin** button.
1. [Plugins menu](<plugins-menu-file-structure.md>) - This menu displays only the activated plugins.
1. [Register plugins](<plugin-register.md>) - Upon purchasing plugin licences, you will receive the licence codes via email along with instructions for activation. Simply add the licence codes provided to access the licenced version of the plugin.
