# Customising document type terminology - Purchase

***

The language associated with each Set of Books determines the specific labels used to cater to different countries or tax regulations. In most languages, the term *'Purchase'* or its equivalent will be displayed. For instance, en-UK and en-South Africa use terminology related to *'Purchase'*, while in South Africa, Afrikaans refers to *'Aankoop'* or *'Aankope'*.

These purchase-related labels are visible within the osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 interface when you access a Set of Books. They are also present in reports and document layout files generated by the system.

To adapt the purchase document type terminology to suit your country, jurisdiction, and industry, you can customize and modify the purchase-related labels via the "*Customize Languages*" screen. This screen is accessible through the **Tools → [Customise Language**](<customise-language.md>) menu on the **Setup** ribbon.

You may also find it helpful to utilize the search options available. This enables you to search for a specific label either in the currently active language file you're editing or in the default "*English*" language file. This assists you in identifying and modifying specific text to better suit your requirements.
