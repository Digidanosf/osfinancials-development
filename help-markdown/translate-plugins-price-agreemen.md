# Price Agreement - Translatable reports &nbsp;

Reports&nbsp; is translatable **Reports → Plugins → Price agreement**. (**Reports** ribbon).

* Debtor price agreement
* Creditor price agreement

**Stock items** (**Default** ribbon) - **Reports** button on sidebar, or **Print single item** context menu option.

* Price list - Large retail - Sales
* Price list - Large retail - Purchases
