# Currencies (Reports menu)

***

The "*Currencies - Listing*" report will list the currencies (added / edited in the **Setup → [Currencies**](<multi-currency-documents.md>) menu on the **Setup** ribbon).&nbsp;

You may process [Multi-currency documents](<multi-currency-documents.md>) (i.e. invoices, credit notes and quotes for debtors (customers / clients) or purchases, supplier returns and orders for creditors (suppliers). The selected currency will be printed in the document layout files.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *osFinancials5/TurboCASH5 supports a wide range of currencies, with a total of 180 available currency rates. As these currency rates can fluctuate daily, and sometimes even hourly, users must ensure they regularly update and maintain their currency list before processing any documents. This ensures that accurate exchange rates are used when dealing with foreign currencies on sales documents (invoices, credit notes, and quotes) or purchase documents (purchases, supplier returns, and orders).* |
| --- | --- |


# Currencies - Listing report options

**To print Currencies listing reports:**&nbsp;

1. On the **Reports** ribbon, select **Reports → Currencies**.&nbsp;

&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; ![Image](<lib/reports-currencies-options.png>)

2. Select the Sequence (i.e. ID (record number), Description or Rate).
2. Click on the **OK** button.&nbsp;
