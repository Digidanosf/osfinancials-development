# Salespersons

***

This option allows you to add / delete the salespersons, who sell your stock items or services. Salespersons may be selected on sales documents (quotes, invoices point-of-sales access and credit notes).

The Salespersons are also added to the purchase documents (orders, purchases and supplier returns). This feature is useful to manage a user / salesperson / cashier who have processed a specific purchase document.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | *You may select the default salesperson for each debtor (customer / client) account. When sales documents are generated and the debtor (customer / client) account is selected, the default salesperson will be displayed for the document or you may select any available salesperson.*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


This feature makes it easy to see who made the most sales or did not make target. It is also a useful tool if it is necessary to pay commission on those sales.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | *Salespersons may also be selected for purchase documents (i.e. Purchases, Supplier returns and Orders). This allows you to see who handled the purchase document.*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | *You may also link the salespersons (cashiers) to users on the **Setup →** **Access control** - **Others** tab ([**Setup***](<setup-ribbon.md>) *ribbon) for improved Point-of-Sale access and security.*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


**To add Salespersons:**

| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | *Salespersons may also be added or deleted in **Setup → Groups - [Salespersons***](<setup-groups-salespersons.md>)*) ([**Setup***](<setup-ribbon.md>) *ribbon).*&nbsp; |
| --- | --- |


1. On the **Setup** ribbon, select **Setup → Salesperson**. The "*Salesperson*" screen is displayed:

![Image](<lib/setup-salesperson 1.png>)

2. Click on the **Add** button. A new record will be inserted "*Change name*"
2. Overtype this with the name of your salesperson. Repeat this process for all your salespersons.&nbsp;
2. Click on the **Close** button.

**To delete Salespersons:**

1. On the **Setup** ribbon, select **Setup → Salesperson**.&nbsp;
1. Select the Salesperson and click on the **Delete** button. A confirmation message is displayed.

*Do you want to delete the salesperson?: Name*

3. Make absolutely sure that the correct salesperson is selected.&nbsp;
3. Click on the **Yes** button to delete the salesperson.&nbsp;
3. Click on the **Close** button.&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-tip.png>) | ***To print a list of the salespersons:*** *Go to **Setup → Groups** ([**Setup***](<setup-ribbon.md>) *ribbon).* *On the **Salespersons** group, click on the **Print** button.*&nbsp; |
