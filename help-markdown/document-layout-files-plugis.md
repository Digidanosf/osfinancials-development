# Kassabon VM&nbsp;

To print this layout file, the HORECAPOS plugin need to be activated and installed.

| ![Image](<lib/B46.png>) | ***HORECAPOS** - [Shop*](<https://www.osfinancials.org/en/webshop/register-system---pos/horeca-pos-plugin> "target=\"\_blank\"") *- Annual subscription -* *With this plugin you can create a checkout system specifically for catering.*&nbsp; *The use of hand-held computers can speed up the serving of customers and gives you a control of the ordered products.*&nbsp; *After recording an order at a table, the ticket printer in the kitchen and/or bar and/or POS pc will be used for the specific printout of the products.* *You can specify a product to be a "kitchen" or "bar" product, and thus the printer specified is used to print the order.*&nbsp; *You can even use product options such as butter, mustard, bread, etc. if the customer chooses that.*&nbsp; *Products can be selected through a quick search function based on the starting letter of the product.*&nbsp; *Or through icon based images of the product group and ultimately the product.*&nbsp; |
