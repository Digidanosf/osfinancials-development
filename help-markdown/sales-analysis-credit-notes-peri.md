# Credit notes per period (Reports menu)

***

The following five (5) reports are available to analyse credit notes:

1. **Credit notes per period - Debtor group 1** - List the credit notes by debtor (customer / client) reporting group 1 linked to debtor (customer / client) accounts on **Debtors** (**Default** ribbon). &nbsp;
1. **Credit notes per period - Debtor group 2** - List the credit notes by debtor (customer / client) reporting group 2 linked to debtor (customer / client) accounts on **Debtors** (**Default** ribbon). &nbsp;
1. **Credit notes per period - Document group 1** - List the credit notes by document reporting group 1. &nbsp;
1. **Credit notes per period - Document group 2** - List the credit notes by document reporting group 2. &nbsp;
1. **Credit notes per period - Salesperson** - List the credit notes by salesperson (person who processed the credit note).&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-important.png>) | *This report only includes Credit notes which are posted. Unposted Credit notes will be **NOT** be included in this report.* *To view a list of unposted documents, which is not updated to the ledger, go to **Input → [Checking unposted items***](<checking-for-unposted-items.md>) *(**Default** ribbon).* |

# Sales analysis - Credit notes per period report options

**To print Credit notes by Reporting groups reports:**&nbsp;

1. On the **Reports** ribbon, select **Reports → Sales analysis → Credit notes per period**.&nbsp;

![Image](<lib/reports-sales-analysis-credit-notes-options.png>)

2. Select the following:
1) &nbsp;

   1) **Report** - Select the Credit notes per period - Debtor group 1 (default), Credit notes per period - Debtor group 2, Credit notes per period - Document group 1, Credit notes per period - Document group 2 or Credit notes per period - Salesperson report.&nbsp;
   1) **Period** - Select the period on the **Per period** tab. You may click on the **Free selection** tab to select a specific date or a range of dates.&nbsp;
   1) **Sequence** - Select "*Debtor code*" to print the report by account code or "*Description*" to print the report by account description (name).
   1) **New page per account** - If this option is selected (ticked), it will print the transactions for each account on a new page.

| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *The Company details will not be printed as in the Page header. The company name is displayed in the Page footer.* |
| --- | --- |


3. Click on the **OK** button.&nbsp;
