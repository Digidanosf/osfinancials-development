# Payroll plugin

| ![Image](<lib/icon-osf-shop-payroll.jpg>) | ***Payroll** - Manual - [Shop*](<https://www.osfinancials.org/en/webshop/other/payroll-plug-in> "target=\"\_blank\"") *- **Licence** : Once-off -* *Supported applications: Logisal, RoosRoos and Unit 4.* |
| --- | --- |


Seamlessly import payroll data from supported applications like Logisal, RoosRoos, and Unit 4 directly into osFinancials5/TurboCASH5, simplifying payroll-related transactions.

Is the program you use not listed? No problem\! With our commitment to customer satisfaction, simply send us an export, and we will explore the possibility of adding that import. In most cases, the addition is free of charge.

Say goodbye to manual data entry and hello to efficiency as you effortlessly generate batches or journals for payroll processing within osFinancials5/TurboCASH5.&nbsp;

**Payroll Plugin Access** - **Batch entry** - **F9:Process** - **Payroll import** option.
