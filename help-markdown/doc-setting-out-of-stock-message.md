# No out of stock message

***

By default, an information message for the trading stock items (Default stock item type) is displayed when the stock item is out of stock (insufficient quantities) or when a quantity is entered in the “*Qty Shipped*” field of the Document entry screen. To hide this message, select (tick) this "*No out of stock message*" option.
