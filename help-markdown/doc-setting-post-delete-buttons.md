# Add Posting and Delete buttons

***

By default, only the **New**, **Edit** and **Print** buttons are available on the Documents grid's sidebar. You may add the **Posting** and **Delete** buttons underneath the **Print** button for a selected user:

* **Posting** - This will allow you to launch the **Update ledger** screen to update (post) documents (Invoices, Credit notes, Purchases and Supplier returns) to the ledger.&nbsp;

| ![Image](<lib/admon-note.png>) | *If Quotes and Orders is selected as the document type, the caption of the **Posting** button, will change to **Confirmation**. If you click on the **Confirmation** button, it will launch the [**Confirm quotes and orders***](<doc-grid-confirm-quotes.md>) *process option on the Documents list screen's context menu.* |
| --- | --- |


* **Delete** - This will allow you to delete an unposted document.

![Image](<lib/documents-post-delete-buttons-default.png>)

If the **Posting** and **Delete** buttons are not available, you may:

1. To post documents, click on the **Update ledger** (**Default** ribbon) or press the **F3** key.
1. To delete documents, click on the **Edit → Delete → Documents** (**Default** ribbon).&nbsp;

![Image](<lib/delete-documents-menu-access.png>)

**To Add the Posting and Delete buttons to the Document list screen:**

1. Click on the **Setup → Access control**.
1. Select the **Documents** tab.&nbsp;
1. Select (tick) the **Document posting** field.

![Image](<lib/documents-post-delete-buttons-setting.png>)

4. Click on the **Apply** button.
4. Click on the **Close** button.
4. You need to click on **Tools → [Close active forms**](<close-active-forms.md>) (**Setup** ribbon) or reopen the Set of Books. The [**Posting**](<update-post-documents.md>) and [**Delete**](<delete-documents.md>) buttons will then be available on the Documents list screen.&nbsp;

![Image](<lib/documents-post-delete-buttons-added.png>)
